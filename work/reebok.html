The Challenge

Post-acquisition by SPARC Group, Reebok needed a complete digital transformation to establish independence while maintaining world-class commerce capabilities.

Since 1895

1 Rapid Platform Launch

Build and deploy a headless commerce platform on aggressive timeline to support the brand transition

2 Flawless Data Migration

Move 3M+ customer records, full product catalogs, and reviews with zero downtime or data loss

3 World-Class Experience

Match Adidas-tier performance and fuel business agility for personalization and growth

The Solution

NULogic delivered a comprehensive MACH architecture solution, custom-built for Reebok's unique requirements and aggressive timeline.

1

Modern Headless Architecture

Core Stack: React front-end, Node.js/GraphQL back-end, MACH framework, AWS cloud infrastructure

Content & Discovery: Bloomreach Experience Manager (CMS), Bloomreach Search & Recommendations, SSR optimization

Product Management: Custom PIM built in-house (saved hundreds of thousands in annual licensing costs)

2

Seamless Checkout & Commerce

Payment Integration: Braintree payments, Forter fraud detection, Fiserv gift cards, Vertex tax calculation

Order Management: Manhattan OMS, Talon.One promotions engine

Marketing: Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Attentive SMS, Bluecore personalization

3

Zero-Downtime Migration

Data Precision: Automated Python scripts processed 3M+ customer records, reviews, and DAM assets

Execution: Scrum-based agile methodology with continuous stakeholder alignment

Support: 24/7 monitoring, AWS DevOps, post-launch optimizations
