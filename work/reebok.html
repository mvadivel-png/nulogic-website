Case Study

Reebok: Zero-Downtime Migration to a Custom MACH Commerce Platform with NULogic

Following acquisition by SPARC Group, Reebok needed a fully independent eCommerce platform. NULogic delivered a seamless migration to a custom MACH architecture with zero downtime.

Region

U.S.

Website

reebok.com

Client Since

2022

Client Overview

Reebok is an iconic and irreverent sports culture brand with a rich and storied fitness heritage dating back to 1895. The brand operates in 80 countries with approximately 400 freestanding stores globally, positioning itself at the intersection of active, lifestyle, and sport segments.

The Challenge

Following acquisition by SPARC Group, Reebok faced these obstacles:

  • Stand up a new, fully functional headless commerce platform within aggressive timelines
  • Migrate millions of customer records, product catalogs, and reviews seamlessly with zero downtime
  • Deliver a next-generation customer experience matching Adidas-run site performance
  • Equip business teams with scalable tools for product launches and personalized engagement

The Solution

NULogic delivered a comprehensive MACH commerce implementation featuring:

Platform Architecture

ReactJS front-end with Node.js/GraphQL back-end using open-source MACH framework

Content & Search

  • Bloomreach Experience Manager (CMS)
  • Bloomreach Search & Recommendations

Custom PIM

Built proprietary Product Information Management system, saving hundreds of thousands of dollars annually in licensing costs

Checkout Integration

  • Experian (validation)
  • Vertex (tax)
  • Braintree (payments)
  • Forter (fraud)
  • Fiserv (gift cards)

Promotions & Marketing

  • Talon.One deployment
  • Salesforce Marketing Cloud
  • Attentive
  • Bluecore connections

Migration Assets

  • BazaarVoice to PowerReviews
  • Cloudinary to Bynder DAM

Customer Data

Processed 3M+ customer records using Python-based automated scripts

Performance

Server-side rendering (SSR) and cloud optimization

Order Management

Manhattan OMS integration

Delivery Method

Scrum-based agile approach with continuous stakeholder alignment

Results

  • On-Time Launch: Platform went live on schedule with zero downtime
  • Seamless Transition: No disruption to accounts, catalogs, or checkout functionality
  • Performance: Significant improvements through SSR and cloud-native optimization
  • Operational Efficiency: Custom PIM streamlined catalog onboarding workflows
  • Scalable Architecture: Modern headless commerce foundation established
  • Business Continuity: Flawless transition safeguarded revenue streams
  • Post-Launch Support: 24/7 monitoring, AWS DevOps, ongoing optimizations provided
