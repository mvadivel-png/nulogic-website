Zero-Downtime MACH Migration for Reebok

Region

U.S.

Website

reebok.com

Client Since

Client

Reebok is an iconic sports and lifestyle brand with heritage dating back to 1895. Operating in 80 countries with 400+ stores, they unite active, lifestyle, and sport segments globally.

The Challenge

Post-acquisition by SPARC Group, Reebok needed:

  • • Rapid Platform Launch: Build and deploy a headless commerce platform on aggressive timeline
  • • Flawless Data Migration: Move 3M+ customer records, full product catalogs, and reviews with zero downtime
  • • World-Class Experience: Match Adidas-tier performance and fuel business agility for personalization

The Solution

Pillar 1: Modern Headless Architecture

Core Stack: React front-end, Node.js/GraphQL back-end, MACH framework, AWS cloud infrastructure

Content & Discovery: Bloomreach Experience Manager (CMS), Bloomreach Search & Recommendations, SSR optimization

Product Management: Custom PIM built in-house (saved hundreds of thousands in annual licensing costs)

Pillar 2: Seamless Checkout & Commerce

Payment Integration: Braintree payments, Forter fraud detection, Fiserv gift cards, Vertex tax calculation, Experian validation

Order & Promotion Management: Manhattan OMS, Talon.One promotions engine

Marketing Connections: Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Attentive SMS, Bluecore personalization

Pillar 3: Zero-Downtime Migration Execution

Data Precision: Automated Python scripts processed 3M+ customer records, reviews (BazaarVoice to PowerReviews), and DAM assets (Cloudinary to Bynder)

Execution: Scrum-based agile methodology with continuous stakeholder alignment

Support: 24/7 monitoring, AWS DevOps, post-launch optimizations

Results

  • • Zero-Downtime Launch: Live on schedule with zero service interruption
  • • Flawless Migration: 3M+ customer records migrated with 100% data integrity
  • • Performance Gains: Achieved Adidas-tier performance through SSR and cloud optimization
  • • Annual Savings: Custom PIM saved hundreds of thousands in licensing costs
  • • Revenue Protected: Seamless transition maintained all revenue streams with zero customer disruption
  • • Scalable Foundation: Modern MACH architecture enables rapid product launches and personalization
  • • Ongoing Excellence: 24/7 AWS DevOps support with continuous optimization
