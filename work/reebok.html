Case Study

Reebok Transforms Digital Commerce with Zero-Downtime MACH Platform Migration

NuLogic partnered with Reebok to execute a seamless migration from legacy monolithic infrastructure to a modern MACH (Microservices, API-first, Cloud-native, Headless) architecture, transforming their digital commerce capabilities without any business disruption.

Client

Reebok

Region

United States

Industry

Athletic Apparel & Footwear

Solution Type

Platform Migration

The Challenge

Following Reebok's acquisition by SPARC Group, the company needed to establish an independent eCommerce platform after being excluded from the Adidas-operated system during the transition. This critical digital transformation required building a fully functional headless commerce platform under aggressive timelines while maintaining zero downtime for millions of global customers. Key challenges included:

  • Aggressive timeline: Standing up a new, fully functional headless commerce platform under strict deadlines with fixed, non-negotiable launch dates
  • Data migration scale: Moving millions of customer records (3M+), product catalogs, and reviews seamlessly without any data loss
  • Zero downtime requirement: Ensuring no performance degradation or service interruption during the transition period
  • Experience parity: Delivering a next-generation customer experience matching or exceeding the previous Adidas-operated site
  • Operational enablement: Equipping business teams with scalable tools for product launches, promotions, and personalized engagement

The Solution

Core Technology Stack

Frontend & Backend Infrastructure:

  • ReactJS frontend with server-side rendering (SSR) for optimal performance and SEO
  • Node.js/GraphQL backend architecture enabling flexible, efficient data retrieval
  • AWS cloud infrastructure with scalable, resilient hosting capabilities

Content & Commerce Management:

  • Bloomreach Experience Manager (CMS) for powerful content management
  • Bloomreach Search & Recommendations for intelligent product discovery
  • Custom-built Product Information Management (PIM) system to streamline product onboarding workflows and reduce licensing costs

Integrated Solutions

Checkout & Payment Processing:

  • Experian for address validation ensuring accurate deliveries
  • Vertex for precise tax calculations across all jurisdictions
  • Braintree for secure, flexible payment processing
  • Forter for advanced fraud protection and risk management
  • Fiserv for comprehensive gift card management

Promotional & Marketing Capabilities:

  • Talon.One promotions engine for complex omnichannel campaign logic and sophisticated promotional strategies
  • Salesforce Marketing Cloud for personalized automation and customer journey orchestration
  • Attentive for SMS/email engagement and multi-channel communication
  • Bluecore for advanced campaign tracking and performance analytics

Order & Inventory Management:

  • Manhattan OMS for centralized order orchestration across all channels
  • BOPIS (buy online, pick up in-store) capabilities for enhanced customer convenience
  • Real-time inventory management integration ensuring accurate stock visibility

Digital Asset & Review Management:

  • Bynder DAM replacing Cloudinary for efficient digital asset storage and management
  • PowerReviews replacing BazaarVoice for comprehensive customer reviews and ratings

Team Structure & Methodology

NULogic assembled a cross-functional team including project managers, solution architects, full-stack developers, QA engineers, DevOps specialists, and business analysts. The team employed scrum-based agile delivery with continuous stakeholder alignment, incremental milestone releases, and iterative development cycles to ensure successful delivery.

Business Impact

  • Flawless transition from Adidas systems, safeguarding revenue streams and business continuity
  • Enhanced operational efficiency with streamlined workflows for product launches and promotions
  • Cost savings through custom PIM development versus expensive third-party licensing
  • Scalable infrastructure supporting long-term growth and peak traffic demands
  • Improved customer experience with faster performance and seamless functionality
