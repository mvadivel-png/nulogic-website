Case Study

Reebok Transforms Digital Commerce with Zero-Downtime MACH Platform Migration

NuLogic partnered with Reebok to execute a seamless migration from legacy monolithic infrastructure to a modern MACH (Microservices, API-first, Cloud-native, Headless) architecture, transforming their digital commerce capabilities without any business disruption.

Client

Reebok

Region

United States

Industry

Athletic Apparel & Footwear

Solution Type

Platform Migration

The Challenge

Reebok's legacy e-commerce platform was limiting their ability to innovate and compete in the fast-paced athletic apparel market. Key challenges included:

  • Monolithic constraints: Tightly coupled legacy architecture slowing down feature development and deployment
  • Scalability limitations: Difficulty handling traffic spikes during product launches and promotional events
  • Zero downtime requirement: Migration had to occur without any business interruption or revenue loss
  • Omnichannel complexity: Need to maintain seamless experiences across web, mobile, and retail touchpoints
  • Time to market: Lengthy development cycles preventing rapid response to market trends

The Solution

MACH Architecture Implementation

  • Designed and implemented microservices-based architecture for maximum flexibility
  • API-first approach enabling seamless integration with third-party services
  • Cloud-native deployment on AWS for scalability and resilience
  • Headless commerce architecture decoupling frontend from backend systems
  • Modern tech stack including React, Node.js, and containerized services

Zero-Downtime Migration Strategy

  • Phased migration approach with parallel running systems
  • Comprehensive testing and validation at each migration stage
  • Blue-green deployment strategy for seamless cutover
  • Real-time monitoring and rollback procedures
  • Data migration and synchronization without customer impact

Composable Commerce Capabilities

  • Best-of-breed commerce components integrated via APIs
  • Flexible product catalog and content management
  • Advanced personalization and recommendation engines
  • Multi-channel order management and fulfillment
