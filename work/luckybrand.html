Client Overview

Lucky Brand is an iconic American denim brand known for its authentic, vintage-inspired clothing. With a heritage rooted in premium craftsmanship and bohemian aesthetics, Lucky Brand operates retail stores and e-commerce channels delivering quality denim, apparel, and accessories to customers nationwide.

The Challenge

Lucky Brand needed reliable, continuous support for their Salesforce Commerce Cloud implementation to ensure optimal performance, minimize downtime, and maintain a seamless customer experience across their digital channels.

The Solution

  • Ongoing enhancements and feature development

The Results
