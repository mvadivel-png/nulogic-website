Case Study

Aeropostale

Continuous eCommerce support and 24/7 Salesforce Commerce Cloud monitoring for a specialty retailer with 500+ stores across the U.S. and Puerto Rico.

Overview

Aeropostale, a specialty retailer founded in 1987 with 500+ stores across the U.S. and Puerto Rico, partnered with NULogic to establish continuous eCommerce support for their Salesforce Commerce Cloud operations. The apparel retailer operates a 24/7 eCommerce presence at aeropostale.com while managing physical retail locations. Their digital operations depend on seamless integration across multiple platforms.

The Challenge

Aeropostale faced several critical challenges in maintaining their digital commerce operations:

  • Risk of revenue loss and customer frustration from downtime, particularly during off-hours
  • Need for reliable oversight of Salesforce Commerce Cloud storefront
  • Requirement to monitor multiple integrations including payments, order management systems, CRM, and marketing platforms
  • Reputational vulnerability from digital commerce interruptions

The Solution

NULogic implemented managed commerce support services featuring:

  • Proactive Monitoring: Continuous Salesforce Commerce Cloud oversight
  • Rapid Incident Response: Dedicated engineers available nights, weekends, and holidays
  • Integration Coverage: Full system monitoring across payment, OMS, CRM, and marketing platforms
  • Global Delivery Model: Nearshore and offshore teams from India and LATAM
  • Stability Focus: Preventive processes to detect and resolve issues before customer impact

Services Delivered

  • CDN optimization
  • Real-time dashboarding and alerting
  • 24/7 monitoring and incident management

Results

  • Always-on support with rapid off-hours incident resolution
  • Maintained peak performance through 24/7 monitoring
  • Reduced internal IT burden, freeing teams for strategic initiatives
  • Enhanced organizational confidence in digital infrastructure
