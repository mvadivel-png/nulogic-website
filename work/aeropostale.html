The Challenge

500+ retail locations across the U.S. and Puerto Rico. Omnichannel fashion retailer blending physical and digital commerce. High-traffic eCommerce platform requiring 100% uptime.

500+ Stores

1 Complex Integration Stack

Payments, OMS, CRM, and marketing systems interconnected. Every integration failure cascades to customer impact.

2 Zero Downtime Requirement

Retail operations running 24/7 across multiple time zones. One hour of outage = significant revenue loss.

3 Internal Capacity Constraints

Limited in-house engineering team stretched thin managing peak seasons and incident response overnight.

The Solution

NULogic deployed a three-pillar monitoring and support architecture.

1

24/7 Proactive Monitoring

Real-time visibility across Salesforce Commerce Cloud, payment processors, OMS, CRM, and marketing integrations with intelligent alerting.

2

Dedicated Response Teams

Round-the-clock eCommerce engineers across nearshore (LATAM) and offshore (India) locations for immediate incident resolution.

3

End-to-End Platform Coverage

Comprehensive health checks spanning every critical system powering the Aeropostale storefront.
