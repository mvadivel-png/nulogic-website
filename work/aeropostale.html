Client Overview

500+ retail locations across the U.S. and Puerto Rico. Omnichannel fashion retailer blending physical and digital commerce. High-traffic eCommerce platform requiring 100% uptime.

The Challenge

Complex Integration Stack: Payments, OMS, CRM, and marketing systems interconnected. Every integration failure cascades to customer impact.

Zero Downtime Requirement: Retail operations running 24/7 across multiple time zones. One hour of outage = significant revenue loss.

Internal Capacity Constraints: Limited in-house engineering team stretched thin managing peak seasons and incident response overnight.

The Solution

NULogic deployed a three-pillar monitoring and support architecture:

  • 24/7 Proactive Monitoring: Real-time visibility across Salesforce Commerce Cloud, payment processors, OMS, CRM, and marketing integrations with intelligent alerting
  • Dedicated Response Teams: Round-the-clock eCommerce engineers across nearshore (LATAM) and offshore (India) locations for immediate incident resolution
  • End-to-End Platform Coverage: Comprehensive health checks spanning every critical system powering the Aeropostale storefront
