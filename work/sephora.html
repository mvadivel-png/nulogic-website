Client Overview

Sephora operates as a prestige beauty retailer with 2,700+ stores across 35 countries, including 500+ locations in the Americas. The company blends luxury retail with digital commerce infrastructure.

The Challenge

Sephora's technology landscape faced fragmentation across multiple vendors managing commercetools platforms, point-of-sale systems, and mobile applications separately, creating bottlenecks, extended timelines, and rising costs.

Solution Approach

NULogic implemented a comprehensive solution to consolidate Sephora's technology vendors and streamline their digital commerce operations:

Enterprise Commerce System Re-architecture

Re-architected enterprise commerce systems for scalability and resilience supporting global expansion.

commercetools Optimization and API Enhancements

Enhanced commercetools through API optimization, integration streamlining, and feature refinement for improved shopping experiences.

Unified POS-eCommerce Integration

Unified POS-ecommerce systems to synchronize pricing, inventory, and fulfillment data across channels.

iOS and Android App Enhancement

Improved iOS and Android applications enabling personalized, high-performance mobile shopping.

Nearshore LATAM Delivery Teams

Integrated nearshore LATAM teams providing agile, cost-effective collaboration.

Measurable Results

NULogic's integrated approach delivered significant improvements across Sephora's digital commerce operations:

25% faster delivery acceleration through nearshore teams and API automation

Optimized cost structure reducing operational expenses while maintaining quality standards

Enhanced omnichannel experience creating seamless customer journeys

Improved cross-functional collaboration improving team alignment

Vendor consolidation achieved reducing complexity through single-partner reliance

"NULogic has been invaluable in accelerating delivery with retail expertise and broad architectural capabilities, enabling vendor consolidation and seamless collaboration."

Rajesh Venkatesan

Vice President, eCommerce Engineering at Sephora
