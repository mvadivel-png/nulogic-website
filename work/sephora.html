Client Overview

Sephora operates as a global beauty retailer spanning 2,700+ stores across 35 countries, including 500+ in the Americas. The company positions itself as a cultural icon blending luxury retail with digital commerce. Founded in France with a U.S. debut in 1998, Sephora emphasizes inclusivity and empowerment through both physical stores and digital platforms including ecommerce and mobile applications.

The Challenge

Sephora's technology infrastructure faced fragmentation across multiple vendors:

  • Commercetools platform powered digital commerce operations
  • Separate Point-of-Sale (POS) systems managed retail locations
  • Distinct mobile applications served iOS and Android users

The fragmentation created "bottlenecks," with complexity mounting, timelines extending, and costs increasing. The company required a partner offering "deep retail DNA" capable of consolidating vendors and unifying customer journeys.

The Solution

NULogic implemented a multi-faceted approach:

Foundation Work

Re-architected enterprise commerce systems for scalability and resilience supporting global expansion

Digital Storefront

Enhanced commercetools through API optimization, integration streamlining, and feature refinement for improved shopping experiences

Omnichannel Integration

Unified POS-ecommerce systems to synchronize pricing, inventory, and fulfillment data across channels

Mobile Enhancement

Improved iOS and Android applications enabling personalized, high-performance mobile shopping

Delivery Teams

Integrated nearshore LATAM teams providing agile, cost-effective collaboration

Results & Metrics

25%

Optimized

Cost Structure reducing operational expenses while maintaining quality standards

Enhanced

Omnichannel Experience creating seamless customer journeys

Team Collaboration improving cross-functional alignment
