Case Study

Sephora Modernizes Enterprise Commerce with Unified Omnichannel Platform

NuLogic partnered with Sephora to consolidate fragmented technology systems, creating a scalable enterprise commerce backbone that unifies 2,700+ stores across 35 countries with seamless digital experiences.

Client

Sephora

Region

United States

Industry

Beauty & Cosmetics

Solution Type

Digital Transformation

The Challenge

Sephora operates 2,700+ stores across 35 countries, with over 500 locations in the Americas. As the beauty retailer expanded, their infrastructure relied on disconnected systems that created significant operational inefficiencies and inconsistent customer experiences:

  • Fragmented vendor landscape: Multiple vendors managing commercetools for digital commerce, separate point-of-sale systems for retail locations, and independent mobile applications for iOS and Android platforms
  • Duplication and complexity: Vendor sprawl meant duplication of effort, delayed execution, and missed opportunities to unify customer journeys across physical and digital touchpoints
  • Timeline delays: Project execution consistently experienced delays throughout implementation
  • Rising operational costs: Increasing expenses from managing multiple vendor relationships while maintaining enterprise-grade quality
  • Bottlenecks preventing unified commerce: System separation created barriers to delivering seamless customer experiences across all channels

The Solution

Beginning in 2022, NuLogic partnered with Sephora to modernize its digital commerce ecosystem and consolidate fragmented technology systems while maintaining the prestige brand experience across omnichannel operations.

Enterprise Commerce Architecture

  • Re-architected enterprise commerce systems to provide scalability and resilience supporting global expansion
  • Created a future-ready, scalable enterprise commerce backbone reflecting Sephora's brand commitment to speed, inclusivity, and customer empowerment
  • Established unified architecture eliminating vendor sprawl and operational inefficiencies

Digital Storefront Enhancement

  • Optimized commercetools headless commerce platform for peak performance
  • Refined APIs and streamlined integrations across the digital ecosystem
  • Enhanced storefront features to improve seamlessness and customer experience

Omnichannel Integration

  • Integrated point-of-sale systems with ecommerce platforms
  • Unified pricing, inventory, and fulfillment data across all touchpoints
  • Ensured consistency whether customers shopped in-store or online

Mobile Excellence

  • Enhanced iOS and Android applications with personalized, high-performance shopping experiences
  • Maintained brand prestige across all mobile devices
  • Delivered seamless native app experiences for beauty discovery and purchase

Nearshore Delivery Model

  • Deployed LATAM-based nearshore teams for agile, cost-effective execution
  • Reduced overhead and eliminated delivery bottlenecks
  • Enabled faster project timelines through API-driven automation

Results

The partnership with NuLogic delivered measurable improvements across delivery speed, cost efficiency, customer experience, and operational effectiveness:

25%

Faster delivery timelines through nearshore teams and API-driven automation

Optimized

Cost structure with reduced operational expenses while maintaining enterprise-grade quality

Enhanced

Omnichannel experience with unified systems creating seamless customer journeys

Greater

Team collaboration through improved cross-functional alignment across departments

Consolidated

Vendor complexity reduced through single-partner reliance for commerce operations

Future-Ready

Scalable enterprise commerce backbone supporting global expansion
