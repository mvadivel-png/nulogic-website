Brooks Brothers Enhances Security and Performance with Cloudflare + Salesforce Commerce Cloud

NULogic implemented a dual CDN strategy with Cloudflare layered over Salesforce's native CDN, strengthening security and performance while eliminating Akamai complexity.

Region

U.S.

Website

brooksbrothers.com

Client Since

Client Overview

Brooks Brothers, a leading global fashion retailer, operates a high-traffic e-commerce platform on Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC) to serve millions of shoppers worldwide. Brooks Brothers is a legendary global lifestyle brand that has shaped American style through fashion innovation, fine quality, exceptional service, and value. Since its founding in 1818, the brand's offerings have grown to more than 30 categories, including tailored clothing, sportswear, outerwear, shoes, accessories, fragrance, and a home collection. The brand is available in 500 freestanding stores, shop-in-shops, and outlets in 45 countries worldwide.

The Challenge

As one of the most iconic apparel retailers, Brooks Brothers required a stronger defense against malicious bot traffic that was inflating traffic volumes, increasing infrastructure costs, and threatening customer experience on their Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC) storefront. Their existing setup, which included Salesforce's native CDN along with Akamai, provided basic caching and delivery capabilities but lacked the advanced bot management, rate-limiting, and edge security features needed to keep pace with modern threats. Brooks Brothers turned to NULogic, their managed commerce partner, to design and implement a streamlined solution that eliminated the need for Akamai, reduced complexity, and significantly strengthened site security while maintaining stability during high-traffic retail events.

The Solution

NULogic implemented a dual-CDN strategy with Cloudflare sitting in front of Salesforce's native CDN, creating a layered approach to security, performance, and reliability while removing Akamai from the stack to simplify operations and reduce cost.

Architecture Flow: Client → Cloudflare (security, edge logic) → Salesforce CDN → SFCC origin

Bot Management & WAF

Leveraged Cloudflare's advanced bot detection, WAF, rate-limiting, and API shielding to block malicious hits before they reached SFCC.

Performance Optimization

Applied Cloudflare's Anycast routing, image optimization, and edge caching for faster global delivery, while Salesforce CDN continued serving static storefront assets.

Resilience & Peak Readiness

Configured Cloudflare's Waiting Room and failover logic to ensure checkout stability during peak shopping events like Black Friday.

Edge Logic & Flexibility

Deployed Cloudflare Workers to enable header rewrites, redirects, and feature toggles without requiring SFCC code changes.

Operational Alignment

Designed a cache-coherency and purge strategy to keep Cloudflare and Salesforce caches synchronized during product and promotion updates.

Vendor Consolidation

Retired Akamai from the architecture, reducing complexity and ongoing infrastructure costs.

Measurable Results

The implementation provided immediate and measurable benefits for Brooks Brothers' digital commerce operations:

Stronger Security

Significantly reduced malicious bot traffic, protecting checkout flows and lowering infrastructure load.

Improved Performance

Faster global page delivery, with lower TTFB and improved cache hit ratios.

Peak Season Resilience

Zero disruption during flash sales and seasonal events thanks to Cloudflare's queueing and traffic shaping.

Operational Flexibility

Business teams gained the ability to test and deploy edge logic without code pushes to SFCC.

Simplified Vendor Landscape

By removing Akamai and consolidating edge services under Cloudflare, Brooks Brothers reduced costs, complexity, and improved supportability.

Confidence in Support

With NULogic's managed commerce retainer, Brooks Brothers benefits from proactive monitoring, enhancements, and global off-hours support to ensure continued success.
