Case Study

Stop Bots. Scale Safely. Sell More.

Cloudflare + Salesforce Commerce Cloud eliminated malicious traffic, enhanced global performance, and removed infrastructure complexity for a legendary luxury retailer.

Region
U.S.
Website
www.brooksbrothers.com
Client Since
2023

The Challenge

Legendary luxury retailer since 1818. Brooks Brothers operates a high-traffic e-commerce platform on Salesforce Commerce Cloud serving millions of global shoppers across 500+ stores in 45 countries.

Since 1818

1 Bot Traffic Overload

Malicious bot traffic inflated metrics, spiked infrastructure costs, and threatened checkout stability during peak seasons

2 Fragmented Infrastructure

Salesforce's native CDN + Akamai delivered basic caching but lacked advanced bot management, WAF, and rate-limiting capabilities

3 Operational Complexity

Multiple vendors and toolchains made it difficult to respond to threats in real-time and maintain stability during flash sales

The Solution

Dual-CDN architecture: Cloudflare in front of Salesforce's native CDN, creating a layered defense against modern threats while eliminating vendor complexity and cost.

1

Advanced Protection

Bot Management (Cloudflare's detection engine) blocked malicious traffic before reaching SFCC. WAF + rate-limiting shielded APIs and checkout flows. API protection prevented credential stuffing and inventory scraping.

2

Global Performance

Anycast routing delivered content from optimal edge locations. Image optimization reduced load times for product photography. Edge caching accelerated response times while Salesforce CDN handled static assets.

3

Operational Excellence

Cloudflare Workers enabled real-time logic (header rewrites, redirects, feature toggles) without SFCC deployments. Waiting Room queued traffic during peak events. Vendor retirement: Removed Akamai entirely.

Results

90%+ Bot Traffic Reduction

Malicious bot traffic eliminated; checkout flows fully protected

40% Faster TTFB

Time-to-First-Byte globally; 95%+ cache hit ratio on edge

Peak Season Reliability

Zero downtime during Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and flash sales with Waiting Room queueing

Operational Speed

Business teams deploy edge logic in minutes, not days—no SFCC code pushes required

Cost & Complexity Reduction

Akamai eliminated; simplified vendor footprint reduced annual infrastructure spend

Peace of Mind

NULogic's managed retainer provides 24/7 proactive support, monitoring, and continuous optimization
