Client Overview

Legendary luxury retailer since 1818. Brooks Brothers operates a high-traffic e-commerce platform on Salesforce Commerce Cloud serving millions of global shoppers across 500+ stores in 45 countries. Trusted for American style. The brand offers 30+ categories—from tailored clothing and sportswear to accessories and home collections. Facing modern threats. Malicious bot traffic inflated costs, threatened customer experience, and exposed gaps in their security posture.

The Challenge

Bot Traffic Overload

Malicious bot traffic inflated metrics, spiked infrastructure costs, and threatened checkout stability during peak seasons.

Fragmented Infrastructure

Salesforce's native CDN + Akamai delivered basic caching but lacked advanced bot management, WAF, and rate-limiting capabilities.

Operational Complexity

Multiple vendors and toolchains made it difficult to respond to threats in real-time and maintain stability during flash sales and seasonal events.

The Solution

Dual-CDN architecture: Cloudflare in front of Salesforce's native CDN, creating a layered defense against modern threats while eliminating vendor complexity and cost.

Architecture Flow: Client → Cloudflare (security, edge logic) → Salesforce CDN → SFCC origin

Pillar 1: Advanced Protection

Bot Management (Cloudflare's detection engine) blocked malicious traffic before reaching SFCC. WAF + rate-limiting shielded APIs and checkout flows. API protection prevented credential stuffing and inventory scraping.

Pillar 2: Global Performance

Anycast routing delivered content from optimal edge locations. Image optimization reduced load times for product photography. Edge caching accelerated response times while Salesforce CDN handled static assets.

Pillar 3: Operational Excellence

Cloudflare Workers enabled real-time logic (header rewrites, redirects, feature toggles) without SFCC deployments. Waiting Room queued traffic during peak events. Cache sync strategy kept Cloudflare and Salesforce in harmony. Vendor retirement: Removed Akamai entirely.

Measurable Results

Security

90%+ reduction in malicious bot traffic; checkout flows fully protected.

Performance

40% faster Time-to-First-Byte globally; 95%+ cache hit ratio on edge.

Peak Season Reliability

Zero downtime during Black Friday, Cyber Monday, and flash sales with Waiting Room queueing.

Operational Speed

Business teams deploy edge logic in minutes, not days—no SFCC code pushes required.

Cost & Complexity

Akamai eliminated; simplified vendor footprint reduced annual infrastructure spend.
