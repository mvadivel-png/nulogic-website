Case Study

Brooks Brothers

Brooks Brothers Enhances Security and Performance with Cloudflare + Salesforce Commerce Cloud. Strategic CDN layering delivers advanced bot protection, improved performance, and operational agility for the iconic retail brand.

Overview

Brooks Brothers, a legendary lifestyle brand founded in 1818, operates 500+ stores across 45 countries, offering 30+ product categories including tailored clothing, sportswear, and accessories. NuLogic partnered with Brooks Brothers to implement a dual-CDN architecture that significantly enhanced their Salesforce Commerce Cloud platform's security, performance, and operational agility.

By placing Cloudflare in front of Salesforce's native CDN and retiring Akamai, we delivered advanced bot protection, improved global performance, and gave the business team the capability to deploy edge logic without code pushes.

"NULogic implementing Cloudflare on top of Salesforce Commerce gave us the advanced bot protection and performance optimization we needed to safeguard our customer experience during peak seasons."

— Todd Treonze, SVP and CIO at Brooks Brothers

The Challenge

Brooks Brothers faced three critical issues with their Salesforce Commerce Cloud e-commerce platform that threatened both customer experience and operational efficiency:

1. Malicious Bot Traffic

Unwanted bot activity inflated traffic volumes, increasing infrastructure costs and degrading customer experience. These bots were creating unnecessary load on the system and impacting legitimate customer interactions.

2. Inadequate Security

Their existing setup combining Salesforce's native CDN with Akamai lacked advanced bot management, rate-limiting, and edge security capabilities. This left Brooks Brothers vulnerable to sophisticated attacks and unable to effectively protect their checkout flows.

3. System Complexity

Multiple vendors created operational complexity and limited flexibility for rapid deployments. The fragmented architecture made it difficult to respond quickly to business needs and security threats.

The Solution

NuLogic implemented a dual-CDN architecture placing Cloudflare in front of Salesforce's native CDN while retiring Akamai. This strategic layering approach provided best-in-class security and performance while maintaining compatibility with SFCC.

Architecture Flow:

Client → Cloudflare (security, edge logic) → Salesforce CDN → SFCC origin

Key Implementation Components

Security Layer

  • Advanced bot detection and WAF (Web Application Firewall)
  • Rate-limiting and API shielding
  • Malicious traffic blocking before reaching SFCC origin

Performance Optimization

  • Anycast routing for global distribution
  • Image optimization at the edge
  • Enhanced caching strategies coordinated between both CDNs

Operational Resilience

  • Cloudflare Waiting Room implementation
  • Failover logic for peak shopping events like Black Friday
  • Cache coherency management between layers

Developer Flexibility

  • Cloudflare Workers deployment for header rewrites and redirects
  • Feature toggles without requiring SFCC code changes
  • Business team capability for edge logic testing

Results & Impact

The implementation delivered measurable improvements across multiple dimensions:

Security

Significantly reduced malicious bot traffic and protected checkout flows from attacks

Performance

Faster global page delivery with lower Time-to-First-Byte (TTFB) and improved cache hit ratios

Peak Season Stability

Zero disruption during flash sales and seasonal events

Operational Agility

Business teams gained edge logic deployment capability without code pushes

Vendor Consolidation

Reduced complexity, costs, and improved supportability by eliminating Akamai

24/7 Support

Proactive monitoring and off-hours global support through NULogic's managed commerce retainer

Services Delivered

  • Dashboarding and Alerting: Real-time monitoring and proactive alerting
  • Monitoring and Support: 24/7 managed services and incident response
