Case Study

UPPAbaby

Consolidating 29 localized websites into 7 global sales channels with Shopware migration, achieving 8-month go-live for premium baby gear brand.

Overview

UPPAbaby is a premium baby gear brand specializing in innovative strollers, car seats, and parenting accessories with global expansion ambitions. The company operates across multiple international markets and required modernized digital commerce infrastructure to support their growing business.

NuLogic partnered with UPPAbaby as a Shopware Platinum Partner to implement a comprehensive platform migration from WooCommerce. This transformation consolidated 29 localized websites into 7 global sales channels, enabling single-step global launch and empowering internal teams to manage their international e-commerce operations more effectively.

The Challenge

UPPAbaby faced several critical limitations with their existing WordPress-based WooCommerce platform:

  • WooCommerce Limitations: The platform required excessive workarounds and custom solutions that slowed innovation
  • Scalability Constraints: Difficulty managing multi-market operations and growing international sales channels
  • Heavy Customization Costs: Continuous jerry-rigging increased expenses and maintenance burden
  • Payment Processing Restrictions: Limited flexibility in selecting global payment partners and supporting B2B transactions

The Solution

After evaluating Salesforce Commerce Cloud, commercetools, and Shopify, UPPAbaby selected Shopware based on three critical criteria:

  • Flexibility: Freedom to choose payment processors and integration partners without vendor lock-in
  • Partnership Model: Shopware's direct investment in platform enhancements at no additional cost
  • Cost-Effectiveness: Enterprise-grade capabilities without typical enterprise pricing

NuLogic designed and implemented a comprehensive Shopware architecture with custom development, ERP integrations, and BlueSnap payment integration to enable flexible B2B payments.

Results Achieved

  • Consolidated 29 localized websites into 7 global sales channels
  • Executed single-step global launch avoiding market-by-market rollout
  • Empowered internal teams with minimal retraining required
  • Enabled flexible B2B payments through BlueSnap integration
  • Improved data collection and analysis for international operations
  • 8-month go-live completion
