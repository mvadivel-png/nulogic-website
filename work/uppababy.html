Client Overview

Premium baby gear innovator going global. Strollers, car seats, and parenting accessories trusted by families worldwide. Expanding internationally. Needed a scalable platform to manage multiple markets with control and flexibility.

Outcome: A unified ecommerce engine built on Shopware. Positioned for growth, international expansion, and total operational agility.

Challenge

  • Data Fragmentation: Workarounds for collecting, analyzing, and optimizing data across international markets
  • Scalability Wall: Multi-market operations and growing global sales channels unsupported by legacy platform
  • Vendor Lock-in: Heavy customization, restricted payment options, no B2B/B2C flexibility, high maintenance costs

Solution

Evaluated SFCC, commercetools, Shopify, and Shopware. Shopware won on three pillars:

  • Flexibility & Control — Choose payment processors, integrations, and partners. No vendor lock-in.
  • True Partnership — Shopware invested in feature enhancements upfront, no additional costs
  • Enterprise Capability, Mid-Market Cost — Global scalability without enterprise price tags

Execution: NULogic Partnership

NULogic (Shopware Platinum Partner) handled architecture design, development, integrations, and launch management. PHP-based architecture enabled UPPAbaby's internal team to quickly own and extend the platform.

Results

By The Numbers

  • 29 → 7: Consolidated 29 localized websites into 7 global sales channels
  • 8 Months: WooCommerce-to-Shopware migration go-live ("smoothest in 15+ years")
  • One Launch: Executed unified global launch instead of market-by-market rollout
  • B2B Ready: Flexible payment processing through BlueSnap for B2B and B2C
  • Data Unified: Complete visibility across global operations

What's Next

  • Optimize headless frontend for speed, performance, and mobile conversion
  • Expand BlueSnap for unified B2B and B2C payments
  • Scale to new international markets
