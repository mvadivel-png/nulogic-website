Client Overview

Walmart Chile operates 375+ stores across multiple brands (Lider, Express de Lider, Lider.cl, Ekono, SuperBodega aCuenta, Central Mayorista). The company leads in retail and e-commerce with AI-powered personalization and omnichannel commerce capabilities spanning grocery, digital transformation, and retail growth across Latin America.

The Challenge

Lider's digital ecosystem requires uninterrupted availability. With 375+ stores, multiple brands, complex legacy systems, and high-volume transaction spikes, traditional IT support proves insufficient. Outdated infrastructure and rising customer expectations demand strategic partnership beyond extra manpower.

The Solution

NULogic provides dedicated Level 2 and Level 3 support services operating from Chile, Mexico, and India. The dedicated support model includes:

  • 24/7 monitoring and troubleshooting of infrastructure and applications
  • Best practices implementation for cloud security, compliance, high performance
  • Root cause analysis to prevent recurring issues
  • Regular upgrades, patches, and legacy system modernization
  • Bug fixes, optimizations, and performance tuning
  • Holiday sales readiness ensuring high-traffic spike resilience
