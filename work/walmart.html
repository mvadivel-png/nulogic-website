NuLogic provides enterprise-grade managed support services for Walmart Chile's critical commerce platform operations, ensuring 24/7 availability and rapid incident resolution across Latin America's competitive retail landscape.

Region

Industry

Services

The Challenge

As one of the largest retailers in Chile, Walmart needed a reliable support partner to manage their complex commerce platform operations. Key challenges included:

  • Round-the-clock availability: Required 24/7/365 support coverage for critical e-commerce operations
  • Rapid incident response: Needed expert L2/L3 support engineers to quickly diagnose and resolve complex technical issues
  • Platform stability: Maintain high uptime and performance across peak traffic periods and promotional events
  • Bilingual support: Required Spanish and English-speaking engineers familiar with LATAM market requirements

The Solution

24/7 L2/L3 Support Services

  • Dedicated team of certified engineers providing round-the-clock platform monitoring and support
  • Multi-timezone coverage with bilingual support teams in LATAM and North America
  • Proactive monitoring and alerting to identify issues before they impact customers
  • Incident management with defined SLAs for response and resolution times

Managed Services Excellence

  • Platform health monitoring and performance optimization
  • Regular maintenance windows for updates and patches
  • Detailed incident reporting and root cause analysis
  • Knowledge base development and documentation
  • Escalation procedures and war room coordination during critical incidents
