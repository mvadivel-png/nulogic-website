Overview

Walmart Chile operates 375+ stores across multiple brands including Lider, Express de Lider, Lider.cl, Ekono, SuperBodega aCuenta, and Central Mayorista. The company has established itself as a leader in AI-powered retail innovation and omnichannel commerce throughout LATAM, combining personalized shopping experiences with scalable technology solutions.

The Challenge

Lider faces pressure to maintain uninterrupted digital commerce availability while managing:

  • 375+ stores with complex legacy systems
  • High-volume transaction spikes during peak retail periods
  • Multiple brands requiring coordinated technical support
  • Outdated infrastructure competing with rising customer expectations
  • Need for strategic modernization beyond traditional IT staffing

The Solution

NULogic provides dedicated Level 2 and Level 3 support services operating from Chile, Mexico, and India. Services include:

  • 24/7 monitoring and troubleshooting of infrastructure and applications
  • Cloud security, compliance, and performance optimization
  • Root cause analysis and prevention of recurring issues
  • Legacy system upgrades, patches, and modernization
  • Bug fixes, optimizations, and performance tuning
  • Holiday sales readiness to handle traffic spikes
