Supercharged Search Drives Revenue for Road Runner Sports

AI-powered discovery transformed customer experience and accelerated sales for America's leading running retailer.

REGION

U.S.

WEBSITE

https://www.roadrunnersports.com

CLIENT SINCE

2009

Client Overview

40 years serving runners nationwide. Industry-leading fit technology trusted by fitness enthusiasts. America's top specialty running retailer, recognized for expert product curation and personalized solutions.

The Challenge

  • Slow Search: Timeouts and slowdowns during peak traffic killed conversion rates.
  • Poor Relevancy: Customers couldn't find what they needed; generic results frustrated shoppers.
  • No Personalization: Legacy system delivered one-size-fits-all experience, missing upsell and engagement opportunities.

The Solution

NuLogic deployed Bloomreach Discovery AI on a composable commerce architecture. Three core pillars drove transformation:

  • AI-Powered Search Engine: Configured advanced algorithms and ranking optimization for lightning-fast, accurate product discovery—even with complex filters during peak traffic.
  • Real-Time Personalization: Deployed dynamic recommendation engine that segments customers by behavior and preferences, delivering tailored experiences that drive conversions.
  • Data & Infrastructure: Migrated and optimized the full product catalog, integrated with existing systems, and implemented rigorous testing (integration, regression, performance, load) with continuous monitoring post-launch.

The Results

Lightning-Fast Search

Zero timeout issues. Complex filters execute in milliseconds, even during peak traffic surges.

Precision Discovery

AI-driven matching increased search accuracy and relevancy, helping customers find the right shoes first time.

Revenue Growth

Personalized recommendations and improved search relevancy boosted conversion rates and average order value across all customer segments.

Reduced Support Load

Streamlined product discovery cut customer service inquiries, improved satisfaction scores, and revealed high-value behavior insights.
