Case Study

Road Runner Sports

Road Runner Sports transformed search and boosted revenue using Bloomreach Discovery

About the Client

Road Runner Sports is a family-owned American ecommerce retailer specializing in running shoes and sports gear. With 40 years of experience, the company has become the leading online destination for runners and fitness enthusiasts seeking injury-free footwear solutions.

Client Since

2009

Region

U.S.

Website

roadrunnersports.com

The Challenge

Road Runner Sports faced three primary obstacles hindering growth:

Performance Limitations

The existing search infrastructure experienced slowdowns and timeouts when processing multiple queries with filters, degrading the shopping experience.

Search Relevancy Issues

Results lacked accuracy, frustrating customers and reducing conversion potential as shoppers struggled locating appropriate products.

Personalization Gaps

The legacy system offered no AI-powered personalization or real-time customer segmentation, delivering generic experiences instead of tailored recommendations.

The Solution

NuLogic deployed a composable commerce architecture leveraging Bloomreach Discovery AI technology. The engagement encompassed:

  • Project management and scope definition
  • Bloomreach Discovery system integration
  • Product catalog data migration
  • AI-driven search algorithm configuration
  • Real-time personalization customization
  • Comprehensive testing protocols (integration, regression, performance, load)

Services Delivered

  • Architecture Design
  • eCommerce Development (Integration, Frontend)
  • Integration Services
  • DevOps
  • QA and Load Testing
