Client Overview

Forever 21 is a global fashion leader serving millions of shoppers. The platform: High-traffic e-commerce on Salesforce Commerce Cloud. The stakes: Every peak season, every minute down costs.

The Challenge

Peak Traffic Vulnerability: Seasonal surges (Black Friday, Cyber Monday) pushed infrastructure to breaking points. Slow load times and checkout failures threatened revenue and brand trust.

Visibility Gap: Without real-time monitoring, issues weren't caught until customers felt them. Every minute of downtime meant lost sales and frustrated shoppers.

Stability at Scale: SFCC platform needed rigorous performance testing, faster releases, and automated safeguards to handle unpredictable demand spikes.

The Solution

NULogic built a three-pillar performance framework combining proactive testing, real-time monitoring, and continuous optimization.

Pillar 1: Stress Test & Validate

  • Black Friday-scale load simulations to expose breaking points
  • Automated testing on every release to catch issues before production
  • CDN and scaling strategies hardened through real-world patterns

Pillar 2: 24/7 Monitoring & Incident Response

  • Real-time visibility across Splunk, New Relic, and SFCC logs
  • Automated anomaly detection with instant alerts to prevent downtime
  • SLA-driven support with failover automation and incident playbooks

Pillar 3: Continuous Optimization

  • SFCC pipeline optimization and database query tuning
  • Infrastructure cost reduction through caching, auto-scaling, and API tuning
  • Seasonal readiness with capacity planning and performance audits

Key initiatives executed:

24/7 Monitoring & Rapid Response

Integrated Splunk, New Relic, SFCC logs with automated alerts. Issues detected and resolved before customer impact.

High-Traffic Load & Stress Testing

Simulated Black Friday/Cyber Monday traffic to uncover breaking points and fine-tune CDN scaling strategies.

System Stabilization & Optimization

Refactored SFCC pipelines, reduced database load, optimized API calls to OMS, CRM, payments.

Proactive, SLA-Driven Support

Deployed automated failover, weekly performance audits, and incident playbooks to guarantee uptime.

The Results

Forever 21 now handles seasonal peaks with confidence. Every traffic spike is managed. Every release is stable. Every customer gets speed.

Key Results:

  • Zero downtime migrations and flawless stability during peak sales events like Black Friday.
  • Weekly production releases with zero disruption to customers or platform performance.
  • Full system visibility with real-time performance dashboards and alerting.
  • Developer productivity surge: Onshore team freed from firefighting; focused on innovation instead.
  • Major cost savings reinvested into customer experience and platform growth initiatives.
