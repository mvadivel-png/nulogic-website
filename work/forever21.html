Client Overview

Forever 21, a leading global fashion retailer, operates a high-traffic e-commerce platform on Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC) to serve millions of shoppers worldwide.

The Challenge

Forever 21 faced mounting pressure to deliver fast, flawless digital experiences on Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC). Seasonal surges like Black Friday and Cyber Monday routinely pushed the limits of their infrastructure, threatening page load speed, checkout reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Without proactive monitoring and rigorous performance testing, even minor disruptions risked revenue loss, customer churn, and damage to brand reputation.

The Solution

NULogic partnered with Forever 21 to design and implement a comprehensive performance framework built on our Modern Commerce, Manage Commerce, and Maximize Commerce pillars.

Modern Commerce:

  • Stress-tested storefronts with simulated Black Friday loads
  • Validated scalability before go-live with proactive load testing
  • Ensured stable launches with automated test coverage

Manage Commerce:

  • 24/7 real-time monitoring with Splunk, New Relic, SFCC logs
  • SLA-driven support and rapid-response incident management
  • Proactive anomaly detection and automated failover strategies

Maximize Commerce:

  • Continuous optimization of speed, scalability, and reliability
  • Seasonal readiness and capacity planning for high-traffic events
  • Infrastructure cost savings through caching, auto-scaling, and tuning

Key initiatives included:

24/7 Monitoring & Rapid Response

Integrated Splunk, New Relic, and SFCC logs with automated alerts, ensuring issues were detected and resolved before impacting users.

High-Traffic Load & Stress Testing

Simulated Black Friday/Cyber Monday traffic patterns to uncover breaking points and fine-tune scaling and CDN strategies.

System Stabilization & Optimization

Refactored SFCC pipelines, reduced database query loads, and optimized API calls to OMS, CRM, and payments.

Proactive, SLA-Driven Support

Introduced weekly audits, knowledge bases, and automated failover strategies to drive uptime and resilience.

The Results

With continuous performance monitoring, proactive optimizations, and real-world load testing, Forever 21 can confidently handle seasonal peaks and unexpected traffic surges while delivering an exceptional digital commerce experience.

Key Results:

  • Zero downtime migrations and flawless site stability during peak sales events
  • Weekly production releases with no disruption to customer experience
  • Increased system visibility and performance metrics
  • Freed up onshore developers by offloading monitoring, testing, and issue resolution
  • Significant expense savings overall, enabling reinvestment into customer experience and growth
