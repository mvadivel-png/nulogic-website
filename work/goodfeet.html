Case Study

The Good Feet Store Achieves 99.99% Uptime with AWS Cloud Modernization

Modernizing technology infrastructure through AWS cloud architecture and system integrations for a specialty retailer with nearly 300 locations.

Region

U.S. & International

Website

goodfeet.com

Client Since

2021

Client Overview

The Good Feet Store is a specialty retailer of premium arch supports founded in 1992. Operating nearly 300 retail locations across the United States and internationally, they provide personally fitted arch supports backed by a Manufacturer's Lifetime Limited Warranty. With more than 400 styles available, The Good Feet Store serves customers seeking solutions for foot pain, back pain, and improved overall mobility.

The Challenge

The Good Feet Store's technology infrastructure required modernization to support their growing retail network and evolving business needs. Their existing systems presented several critical challenges:

  • Legacy Infrastructure: Outdated on-premise systems lacking scalability and flexibility for multi-location operations
  • Limited System Integration: Disconnected systems hindering data flow between retail locations and corporate systems
  • Reliability Concerns: Need for enterprise-grade uptime to support business-critical operations across 300 locations
  • Scalability Requirements: Infrastructure unable to efficiently scale with business growth and seasonal demand fluctuations

The Solution

NuLogic partnered with The Good Feet Store to design and implement a comprehensive AWS cloud modernization strategy, transforming their technology infrastructure to support their multi-location retail operations.

AWS Cloud Architecture

NuLogic designed a robust, scalable cloud architecture leveraging AWS's most reliable services:

  • Multi-AZ Architecture: High-availability deployment across multiple AWS Availability Zones for maximum uptime
  • AWS Lambda & API Gateway: Serverless computing for scalable, event-driven application logic
  • Amazon RDS: Managed relational database with automated backups and failover capabilities
  • Elastic Load Balancer: Automatic traffic distribution for optimal performance and reliability
  • Amazon CloudWatch: Comprehensive monitoring and alerting for proactive issue resolution
  • Amazon S3: Scalable object storage for business-critical data and assets

System Integrations

Seamless integration of critical business systems to enable unified operations:

  • Salesforce CRM Integration: Centralized customer data and engagement tracking across all retail locations
  • Erply POS Integration: Real-time point-of-sale data synchronization for inventory and sales management
  • Appointedd Integration: Streamlined appointment scheduling system for personalized fitting consultations
  • Verkada Integration: Modern cloud-managed security camera system for store operations

Quality Assurance & Testing

Comprehensive load testing and QA processes ensured the new infrastructure could handle peak traffic and business-critical operations with confidence.
