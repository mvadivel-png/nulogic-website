Case Study

Wild Fork Foods Scales Specialty Food Retail with commercetools Migration

NuLogic enabled Wild Fork Foods' transition from Shopify Plus to commercetools, delivering enterprise composable commerce capabilities that support complex inventory management, subscription services, and rapid multi-market expansion.

Client

Wild Fork Foods

Region

LATAM & North America

Industry

Specialty Food Retail

Services

Platform Migration & Build

The Challenge

Wild Fork Foods, a rapidly growing specialty food retailer offering premium meats, seafood, and prepared foods, faced significant operational constraints with their Shopify Plus platform. As they expanded their retail footprint and e-commerce operations across multiple markets, they needed a more flexible, scalable solution. Key challenges included:

  • Platform limitations: Shopify Plus couldn't support complex inventory management requirements for perishable goods with specific storage and handling needs
  • Subscription complexity: Limited flexibility for managing recurring subscription orders with customizable delivery schedules and product variations
  • Multi-market expansion: Needed to rapidly launch in new markets with localized pricing, payment methods, and fulfillment capabilities
  • Integration constraints: Difficulty integrating with specialized cold-chain logistics providers and warehouse management systems
  • Customization barriers: Limited ability to create unique shopping experiences and promotional mechanics for specialty food products
  • Omnichannel requirements: Need to unify online ordering with in-store inventory and pickup/delivery options across growing store network

The Solution

Strategic Migration Planning & Execution

  • Comprehensive assessment of existing Shopify Plus implementation and business requirements
  • Detailed migration roadmap with phased approach to minimize business disruption
  • Data migration strategy ensuring zero data loss for customer accounts, order history, and product catalogs
  • Parallel environment testing and validation before cutover
  • SEO preservation strategy maintaining search rankings and URL structures

commercetools Composable Commerce Architecture

  • API-first, headless architecture providing flexibility for custom frontend experiences
  • Microservices-based design enabling independent scaling of critical commerce functions
  • Custom product catalog structure supporting complex SKU variations, bundle configurations, and perishability tracking
  • Advanced pricing engine with support for dynamic pricing, promotions, and customer-specific pricing tiers
  • Multi-currency and multi-language capabilities for LATAM and North American markets

Advanced Inventory & Fulfillment Integration

  • Real-time inventory synchronization across online and physical store locations
  • Integration with warehouse management systems (WMS) for cold-chain logistics
  • Custom fulfillment logic supporting delivery time windows and temperature-controlled shipping
  • Store-level inventory visibility for buy-online-pickup-in-store (BOPIS) capabilities
  • Automated inventory allocation and replenishment workflows

Subscription & Customer Experience Enhancement

  • Flexible subscription management with customizable delivery frequencies and product swaps
  • Customer portal for subscription management, payment updates, and delivery preferences
  • Personalized product recommendations based on purchase history and preferences
  • Enhanced checkout experience with multiple payment options and saved payment methods
  • Loyalty program integration with points accrual and redemption
