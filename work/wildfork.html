Wild Fork Foods Scales Specialty Food Retail with commercetools Migration

NuLogic enabled Wild Fork Foods' transition from Shopify Plus to commercetools, delivering enterprise composable commerce capabilities that support complex inventory management, subscription services, and rapid multi-market expansion.

Region

LATAM & North America

Website

wildforkfoods.com

Client Since

Client Overview

Wild Fork Foods is a rapidly growing specialty food retailer offering premium meats, seafood, and prepared foods through a hybrid retail model combining physical stores with robust e-commerce operations. With an expanding footprint across North America and LATAM markets, Wild Fork needed a commerce platform that could support complex inventory management for perishable goods, flexible subscription services, and rapid multi-market expansion while providing the customization capabilities required for their unique business model.

The Challenge

Wild Fork Foods faced significant operational constraints with their Shopify Plus platform as they scaled their retail footprint and e-commerce operations across multiple markets. The limitations of Shopify Plus were preventing them from executing their growth strategy and delivering the customer experience they envisioned.

Platform Limitations

Shopify Plus couldn't support complex inventory management requirements for perishable goods with specific storage and handling needs, creating operational inefficiencies and limiting their ability to optimize stock management.

Subscription Complexity

Limited flexibility for managing recurring subscription orders with customizable delivery schedules and product variations meant they couldn't fully capitalize on their subscription business model.

Multi-Market Expansion

Needed to rapidly launch in new markets with localized pricing, payment methods, and fulfillment capabilities, but Shopify Plus made this process slow and resource-intensive.

Integration Constraints

Difficulty integrating with specialized cold-chain logistics providers and warehouse management systems critical for their perishable food operations.

Customization Barriers

Limited ability to create unique shopping experiences and promotional mechanics specifically designed for specialty food products and their diverse customer segments.

Omnichannel Requirements

Need to unify online ordering with in-store inventory and pickup/delivery options across growing store network to provide seamless customer experiences.

The Solution

NuLogic designed and executed a comprehensive migration from Shopify Plus to commercetools, implementing a modern composable commerce architecture that provides Wild Fork Foods with the flexibility, scalability, and capabilities needed to support their ambitious growth plans.

Strategic Migration Planning & Execution

  • Comprehensive assessment of existing Shopify Plus implementation and business requirements
  • Detailed migration roadmap with phased approach to minimize business disruption
  • Data migration strategy ensuring zero data loss for customer accounts, order history, and product catalogs
  • Parallel environment testing and validation before cutover
  • SEO preservation strategy maintaining search rankings and URL structures

commercetools Composable Commerce Architecture

  • API-first, headless architecture providing flexibility for custom frontend experiences
  • Microservices-based design enabling independent scaling of critical commerce functions
  • Custom product catalog structure supporting complex SKU variations, bundle configurations, and perishability tracking
  • Advanced pricing engine with support for dynamic pricing, promotions, and customer-specific pricing tiers
  • Multi-currency and multi-language capabilities for LATAM and North American markets

Advanced Inventory & Fulfillment Integration

  • Real-time inventory synchronization across online and physical store locations
  • Integration with warehouse management systems (WMS) for cold-chain logistics
  • Custom fulfillment logic supporting delivery time windows and temperature-controlled shipping
  • Store-level inventory visibility for buy-online-pickup-in-store (BOPIS) capabilities
  • Automated inventory allocation and replenishment workflows

Subscription & Customer Experience Enhancement

  • Flexible subscription management with customizable delivery frequencies and product swaps
  • Customer portal for subscription management, payment updates, and delivery preferences
  • Personalized product recommendations based on purchase history and preferences
  • Enhanced checkout experience with multiple payment options and saved payment methods
  • Loyalty program integration with points accrual and redemption
