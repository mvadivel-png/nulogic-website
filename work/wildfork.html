Scaling Specialty Food Retail at Speed

Wild Fork Foods escaped Shopify Plus constraints through commercetools migration, unlocking enterprise composable commerce for complex inventory, subscriptions, and multi-market expansion.

Region

LATAM & North America

Website

wildforkfoods.com

Client Since

2024

Client Overview

Premium specialty food retailer operating across North America and LATAM. Hybrid model: physical stores + e-commerce. Explosive growth with expanding market footprint. Demanded complex inventory management for perishables, flexible subscriptions, and rapid multi-market scaling. Shopify Plus couldn't keep pace.

The Challenge

Shopify Plus hit its ceiling fast. Rigid platform, scaling barriers, limited customization. Growth strategy blocked.

Complex Inventory & Cold Chain

Perishable goods require precise tracking, storage conditions, and handling. Shopify Plus inventory system designed for standard goods—not temperature-controlled logistics, batch expiration tracking, or location-based fulfillment.

Subscription & Recurring Revenue

Subscriptions are core to Wild Fork's model: flexible frequencies, product swaps, pause/resume functionality. Shopify Plus subscriptions were rigid, inflexible, and required expensive custom workarounds.

Multi-Market Localization

Expanding to 5+ markets meant localized pricing, payment methods, fulfillment networks, and regulatory compliance. Shopify Plus deployment per market = expensive, slow, and operationally fragmented.

The Solution

Complete platform migration to commercetools—a composable commerce engine built for flexibility and scale. Three strategic pillars unlocked growth.

Intelligent Inventory & Fulfillment

Real-time synchronization across stores and warehouses. WMS integration with cold-chain logistics. Custom fulfillment logic for temperature-controlled shipping, delivery windows, and perishability tracking. BOPIS (buy-online-pickup-in-store) capabilities. Automated allocation and replenishment.

Flexible Subscription & Personalization Engine

Fully customizable subscription management: frequencies, product swaps, pause/resume. Customer self-service portal for subscriptions and preferences. Personalized recommendations based on purchase history. Multiple payment options, loyalty integration, and seamless recurring billing.

Enterprise Composable Architecture

API-first, headless design for frontend flexibility. Microservices for independent scaling. Advanced product catalog supporting complex SKUs, bundles, and attributes. Multi-currency, multi-language, multi-market support. Dynamic pricing engine with promotional capabilities.
