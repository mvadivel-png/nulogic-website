Tottus Replatforms from ATG to commercetools on Google Cloud for Scalable Digital Commerce

Region

Latam

Website

Client Since

Client Overview

Tottus, part of the Falabella Group and founded in 2002 in Peru, expanded to Chile as a leading supermarket chain. The company combines robust physical store presence, innovative last-mile delivery application (Tottus App), and e-commerce platform for omnichannel retail. They offer products from global and local brands with emphasis on proprietary lines.

Challenge

Tottus needed modernization of digital commerce operations. Their legacy Oracle ATG platform running on-premise was costly to maintain, lacked agility, and restricted scalability. This infrastructure hindered innovation and multi-brand, multi-channel retail support.

Required replatforming to cloud-native, API-first headless commerce that would:

  • Support multi-tenant, multi-channel commerce across brands and regions
  • Integrate seamlessly with back-office systems including PIM, supply chain, product catalogs, and order management
  • Deliver faster, SEO-optimized customer experiences through modern frontend technology
  • Enable flexible third-party service connections via APIs

Solution

NULogic provided planning, architectural design, and execution for the replatforming program, ensuring seamless "big-bang" migration from Oracle ATG to commercetools and legacy infrastructure to Google Cloud Platform (GCP).

Cloud Migration

Transitioned from on-prem datacenter to GCP for scalability, resilience, and cost efficiency

Headless Architecture

Implemented commercetools with Node.js backend (Fastify) and React/Next.js frontend

System Integrations

Connected PIM, supply chain, product catalogs, and order management systems

Multi-Tenant Enablement

Designed extensible architecture supporting multiple brands and channels

Third-Party Integrations

Enabled payments, email, and additional services through commercetools APIs

Frontend Performance

Optimized for SEO, page load speed, and routing using code splitting

Results

Seamless Migration

Smooth "big-bang" transition from Oracle ATG to commercetools with minimal disruption

Cloud-Native Foundation

Leveraged GCP for greater scalability, higher uptime, and improved cost efficiency

Enhanced Customer Experience

Faster site performance, optimized SEO rankings, improved first-page load speeds

Operational Efficiency

Streamlined workflows by integrating back-office systems, PIM, supply chain, product catalogs, and order management

Future-Ready Flexibility

Enabled support for multiple brands and channels with foundation for continued expansion
