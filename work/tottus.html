Case Study

Tottus

Complete digital commerce transformation migrating from legacy Oracle ATG to modern, cloud-native headless architecture on Google Cloud Platform.

Overview

NULogic partnered with Tottus, a leading Latin American supermarket chain, to execute a complete digital commerce transformation. The initiative involved migrating from legacy Oracle ATG infrastructure to a modern, cloud-native headless architecture.

About Tottus

Tottus, founded in 2002 in Peru and expanded to Chile, operates as part of the Falabella Group. The company blends physical retail stores with innovative digital channels, including a last-mile delivery app and e-commerce platform, serving as a key player in Latin American retail with global and local brand offerings.

Key Challenges

The organization faced several critical constraints that required a comprehensive modernization approach:

Legacy Infrastructure

On-premise Oracle ATG platform created high maintenance costs and lacked scalability for modern commerce demands.

Business Complexity

Required support for multi-brand, multi-channel operations across different regions.

Integration Gaps

Legacy systems couldn't seamlessly connect with PIM, supply chain, catalogs, and order management.

Speed-to-Market

Outdated infrastructure restricted innovation velocity and SEO optimization capabilities.

Solutions Implemented

Cloud Infrastructure

Migration to Google Cloud Platform enabled scalability, resilience, and cost optimization compared to on-premise data centers.

Headless Commerce Architecture

  • Commercetools backend for API-first commerce
  • Node.js/Fastify backend services
  • React/Next.js frontend for performance and SEO

System Integration

Connected back-office systems, PIM, supply chain, product catalogs, and order management through commercetools APIs, enabling flexible third-party service integration including payments, email, and loyalty systems.

Multi-Tenant Enablement

Designed extensible infrastructure supporting multiple brands and channels within a unified ecosystem.

Results Achieved

Seamless Migration

Executed "big-bang" transition with minimal business disruption.

Performance Gains

Enhanced site speed, optimized SEO rankings, and improved first-page load times.

Operational Efficiency

Streamlined workflows across integrated backend systems.

Strategic Flexibility

Created foundation for continued expansion and multi-channel innovation.
