Client Overview

Tottus is Latin America's leading omnichannel supermarket chain. Part of the Falabella Group, they seamlessly blend physical retail with digital innovation. Their Tottus App and e-commerce platform serve millions across Peru and Chile. They power commerce for global and proprietary brands at massive scale.

Challenge

Technical Debt: Legacy Oracle ATG running on-premise was costly, inflexible, and couldn't scale with their growth across regions and brands.

Integration Gaps: Back-office systems (PIM, supply chain, orders) operated in silos. No unified omnichannel vision.

Performance Bottlenecks: Heavy monolith couldn't deliver fast, SEO-optimized experiences their customers demanded.

Solution

We orchestrated a seamless "big-bang" migration from Oracle ATG to a cloud-native, API-first platform. The result: a scalable, future-proof commerce engine.

Pillar 1: Headless Commerce Engine

commercetools on Google Cloud replaced legacy ATG. Built Node.js/Fastify backend with React/Next.js frontend for speed and SEO.

Pillar 2: Unified Backend Systems

Integrated PIM, supply chain, catalogs, and order management into one intelligent backbone. Real-time sync across channels and regions.

Pillar 3: Multi-Tenant, API-First Architecture

Multi-brand support across Peru and Chile. Payment, email, and third-party services plugged in via APIs. Infinite scale.

Results

Zero Downtime Migration

Seamless "big-bang" cutover from Oracle ATG with zero customer impact.

50%+ Cost Savings

GCP replaced expensive on-prem infrastructure. Massive OpEx reduction.

2x Faster Page Loads

Modern Next.js/React frontend + code splitting delivered SEO gains and improved conversions.

Real-Time Inventory Sync

PIM, catalogs, and supply chain now unified. No more silos across Peru and Chile operations.

Unlimited Multi-Channel Scale

Extensible multi-tenant API architecture ready for new brands and channels.
