Scaling Mobility ADO from physical terminals to digital-first commerce. Built high-performance apps handling 280+ concurrent users per minute generating $4M USD monthly.

Client Overview

Mobility ADO is one of Mexico's largest transportation networks. They needed to shift from physical ticketing to a mobile-first platform to reduce terminal congestion, scale ticket sales, and unlock new revenue streams through digital channels and affiliate networks.

The Challenge

Legacy Terminal Operations: Congested physical ticketing at major hubs limited passenger throughput and passenger experience.

Zero Digital Revenue: No mobile or online sales capabilities meant lost revenue opportunities and customer abandonment.

Enterprise Integration Complexity: Multiple backend systems (Oracle Cloud, Huawei, ERPCO) required seamless integration without disrupting operations.

The Solution

Pillar 1: Mobile-First Commerce

Native iOS & Android apps engineered for high-volume transaction processing. Built with 280+ concurrent user capacity per minute, zero downtime, and intuitive UX designed for rapid ticket purchases at scale.

Pillar 2: Enterprise Integration Architecture

Microservices-based backend bridging Oracle Cloud, Huawei Cloud, ERPCO, and APCD systems. Real-time Firebase Analytics for decision-making. Affiliate user roles enabling remote sales points across the network.

Pillar 3: Continuous Excellence

Ongoing project management, QA testing, and maintenance support. Proactive monitoring and optimization to keep the platform running at peak performance.

Results

  • $4M USD monthly revenue generated through the digital platform.
  • 280+ concurrent transactions per minute processed with zero downtime.
  • Terminal congestion reduced by shifting passengers to mobile channels.
  • Real-time analytics enabling data-driven business decisions.
  • Affiliate network expanded to new remote sales points across Mexico.

Impact & Vision

Mobility ADO transformed from a terminal-bound operation into a digital-native transportation leader. The platform now positions them to scale across Latin America, optimize network operations, and dominate the mobile ticketing space.
