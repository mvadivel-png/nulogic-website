Case Study

Mobility ADO

High-performance native mobile applications enabling digital bus ticket purchases and reducing queues at Mexico's busiest transportation terminals.

Overview

NULogic modernized Mobility ADO's ticketing systems by developing high-performance native mobile applications for Android and iOS, enabling customers to purchase digital bus tickets via mobile devices and reducing queues at Mexico's busiest transportation terminals.

Client Background

Company: Mobility ADO (one of Mexico's largest transportation companies)

Region: LATAM

Client Since: 2021

Website: www.mobilityado.com

Mobility ADO sought to accelerate its digital transformation journey through innovative mobile solutions for passenger engagement and operational efficiency.

The Challenge

  • Implement a mobile bus ticket booking solution for digital ticket purchases
  • Improve passenger experience and reduce long queues at major terminals
  • Create a middleware layer to expose internal services to external enterprise applications
  • Enable analytics capabilities
  • Support remote sales through new user roles
  • Handle high transaction volumes during peak travel times

Solutions Implemented

Native Mobile App Development

Built high-performance Android and iOS applications for streamlined online bus ticket booking

Scalable Architecture

  • Designed systems handling 280 concurrent users per minute during peak periods
  • Implemented microservices architecture for modular, cloud-native approach

Backend Integration

Connected with critical systems: APCD, Oracle Cloud, ERPCO, and Huawei Cloud Services

Data & Analytics

Integrated Firebase Analytics for real-time reporting and data-driven decision-making

New Features

  • Introduced affiliate user role enabling agents to sell tickets from remote locations
  • Ongoing support, QA testing, maintenance, and project management

Results Achieved

  • High-volume transaction capacity without downtime
  • Reduced saturation in physical terminals
  • Generated up to $4 million USD per month through the platform
  • Accelerated decision-making via integrated analytics
  • Expanded commercial reach through remote sales points
