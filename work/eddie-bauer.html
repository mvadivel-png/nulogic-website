Region

Website

Client Since

Client Overview

Built for the outdoors. 100+ years of heritage in apparel, footwear, and gear.

Omnichannel presence. 200+ stores across the U.S. and international markets.

Growth-focused. Modernizing digital commerce to compete at scale.

The Challenge

Legacy Architecture. Outdated ATG framework creating scalability bottlenecks and technical debt.

Payment Failures. Authorization issues in checkout flow directly impacting revenue.

Infrastructure Complexity. High maintenance overhead and operational costs limiting agility.

The Solution

Pillar 1: Composable Architecture

Built MACH-based platform with Node.js, React, GraphQL, Python, and MongoDB for flexible integrations and rapid feature deployment. API-first approach replaced legacy monolith.

Pillar 2: Cloud Infrastructure

Migrated to AWS CloudFront with Lambda functions optimized for ARM64 architecture. Zero-downtime deployment ensuring continuous service availability.

Pillar 3: Observability & Operations

Implemented DataDog and Google Tag Manager for real-time monitoring, enabling data-driven optimization and operational intelligence across the platform.

The Results

  • 60% Annual OPEX Savings through cloud optimization and operational efficiency.
  • 32% Infrastructure Cost Reduction via ARM64 Lambda architecture transition.
  • Weekly Deployment Cycle enabling rapid feature releases and business agility.
  • Zero Downtime Migration ensuring continuous availability during cloud transition.
