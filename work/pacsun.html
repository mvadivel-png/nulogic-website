Client Overview

Founded in 1980, PacSun is the destination for youth culture and streetwear enthusiasts. Operating across physical and digital channels, the brand curates emerging brands and contemporary fashion for a digitally-native customer base. They demanded faster innovation velocity to stay competitive in the rapidly evolving streetwear market.

The Challenge

  • Engineering Bottleneck: Limited development capacity slowing time-to-market for critical platform enhancements and payment integrations.
  • Cost Pressure: Rising onshore resource expenses eating into margins while business demanded faster delivery across SFCC and OMS ecosystems.
  • Competitive Urgency: Market velocity required immediate access to specialized Salesforce and order management expertise without lengthy hiring cycles.

The Solution

  • Flexible Talent Model: Blended onshore-offshore engineers embedded directly into PacSun's development teams, scaling resources up and down with business demand.
  • Commerce Cloud Expertise: Specialized engineers with deep SFCC and Manhattan OMS mastery delivered integration work, platform enhancements, and operational support simultaneously.
  • High-Impact Delivery: Launched Klarna payment integration on schedule, enabling flexible checkout options and driving conversion lift across the platform.

The Results

1. On-Schedule Platform Delivery

Klarna integration launched on timeline, expanding payment flexibility and checkout conversion for PacSun customers.

2. 24/7 Omnichannel Coverage

Unified support across SFCC and Manhattan OMS eliminated handoffs, enabling seamless end-to-end commerce operations with zero platform downtime.

3. 40% Lower Resource Costs

Blended onshore-offshore model reduced engineering spend while maintaining enterprise-grade quality and development velocity.

4. 50% Faster Feature Time-to-Market

Flexible resource scaling enabled parallel initiative execution, accelerating innovation cycles for competitive streetwear market demands.
