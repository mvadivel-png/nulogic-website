Case Study

PacSun

Strategic staff augmentation partnership delivering scalable eCommerce engineering support for digital commerce innovation and operational excellence.

Overview

NuLogic partnered with PacSun, a specialty fashion retailer focused on youth culture and digital commerce, to accelerate eCommerce innovation through strategic staff augmentation beginning in 2024. Through a blended onshore-offshore delivery model, we provided scalable eCommerce engineering support that enabled faster development cycles, cost optimization, and operational agility.

Our staff augmentation model offered flexible resourcing that allowed PacSun to scale up or down based on project demand, minimizing onboarding time and avoiding operational disruption. Early high-impact initiatives focused on the successful Klarna integration, which enabled flexible alternative payment options and significantly improved the customer checkout experience.

The Challenge

PacSun faced several critical obstacles that were limiting their ability to innovate and scale:

  • Engineering bandwidth constraints limiting development velocity
  • Rising resource costs impacting profitability
  • Pressure to launch features quickly while maintaining stability
  • Need for faster development cycles within Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC) and Manhattan Order Management System (OMS)
  • Requirement to balance scalability with operational flexibility

Our Solution

NuLogic implemented a comprehensive approach featuring:

  • Staff Augmentation Model: Flexible resourcing allowing scaling up or down based on project demand, minimizing onboarding time and avoiding operational disruption
  • High-Impact Initiatives: Successful Klarna payment integration enabling flexible alternative payment options and significantly improving the customer checkout experience
  • Cross-Platform Coverage: Engineering teams supporting both SFCC and Manhattan OMS, ensuring seamless operations and backend scalability
  • Blended Delivery Model: Strategic onshore-offshore resourcing to reduce total cost of ownership while maintaining quality

Measurable Results

  • Accelerated Delivery: Klarna integration launched on schedule, expanding payment options for customers
  • Platform Expansion: Comprehensive support across SFCC and Manhattan OMS systems
  • Cost Efficiency: Strategic offshore resourcing reduced total cost of ownership
  • Operational Agility: Multiple omnichannel retail initiatives executed in parallel with minimal lead time

"NULogic has become an extension of our team-agile, reliable, and committed to our success."

Shirley Gao, Chief Digital and Information Officer, PacSun
