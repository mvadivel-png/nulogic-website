Client Overview

PacSun is a purpose-driven specialty fashion retailer specializing in youth culture and digital commerce, curating emerging fashion brands, streetwear, activewear, and contemporary clothing. Founded in 1980 in Newport, California, the company operates both physical locations and eCommerce platforms, serving a digitally-native customer base with an emphasis on authentic brand experiences and seamless omnichannel retail.

The Challenge

As PacSun continued to invest in its digital commerce capabilities, the brand faced three primary obstacles:

  • Engineering bandwidth constraints limiting development velocity
  • Rising resource costs impacting profitability and operational efficiency
  • Need for faster feature launches and platform enhancements under increasing market pressure

The company required a strategic partner to accelerate digital innovation within Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC) and Manhattan Order Management System (OMS) while maintaining operational stability and customer experience quality.

The Solution

NULogic implemented a strategic staff augmentation model featuring:

  • Blended onshore-offshore delivery approach optimizing cost and expertise
  • Seamless integration into PacSun's existing workflows and development processes
  • Deep expertise in Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC) and Manhattan Order Management System
  • High-impact initiative: successful Klarna integration enabling flexible payment options for customers
  • Flexible resourcing model enabling demand-based talent scaling across multiple workstreams

This approach allowed PacSun to maintain development momentum while controlling costs and accessing specialized commerce engineering talent on demand.

The Results

Through NULogic's strategic engagement, PacSun achieved measurable improvements across multiple dimensions:

1. Accelerated eCommerce Delivery

The Klarna integration was successfully launched on schedule, expanding alternative payment options and improving the overall checkout experience for PacSun's customer base.

2. Expanded Platform Coverage

Cross-functional teams now provide comprehensive support for both Salesforce Commerce Cloud and Manhattan Order Management System, ensuring seamless end-to-end operations.

3. Cost-Efficient Offshore Resourcing

Strategic offshore delivery models reduced total cost of ownership while maintaining high service quality and development standards.

4. Enhanced Digital Agility

The flexible resource scaling model enabled parallel execution of multiple omnichannel retail initiatives with minimal lead time, significantly improving time-to-market for new features.
