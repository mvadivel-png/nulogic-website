Transforming 135+ years of retail heritage with cloud-native architecture, enabling Falabella to serve 36M+ customers across 9 countries with modern, scalable e-commerce infrastructure.

Client Overview

Falabella is one of Latin America's largest and most prestigious retail conglomerates, with a history spanning over 135 years. The company operates across 9 countries, serving more than 36 million customers and employing 90,000 people throughout the region.

The Falabella portfolio includes leading retail brands such as Falabella department stores, Sodimac (home improvement), and Tottus (supermarkets). The company also operates Banco Falabella, Mallplaza shopping centers, and holds the exclusive partnership for IKEA in Latin America, making it a dominant force in retail innovation and customer experience.

The Challenge

Falabella's legacy Oracle ATG platform created significant obstacles that threatened the company's ability to compete in the rapidly evolving e-commerce landscape:

Outdated Tech Stack

The aging ATG framework lacked the flexibility and modern agility required for contemporary e-commerce demands, limiting innovation and slowing time-to-market for new features.

Environment Management Issues

Critical configuration files lived on servers without version control, creating operational risks and making it difficult to track changes or roll back problematic deployments.

Knowledge Silos

Core architectural knowledge was concentrated with few individuals, creating dependency risks and bottlenecks that hindered development velocity and operational resilience.

Rising Costs

Expensive infrastructure maintenance compared to cloud-native alternatives resulted in high operational costs without delivering proportionate business value.

The Solution

NULogic implemented a comprehensive cloud-native transformation strategy that addressed each challenge while minimizing disruption to Falabella's ongoing operations:

  • Scalable virtualized environments using Azure and Docker for lower environments, providing a reliable and flexible foundation for modern e-commerce operations.
  • Environment-as-Code approach enabling sprint-based development environment creation, eliminating configuration drift and dramatically improving developer productivity.
  • Iterative phased execution with frequent client validation and collaborative planning, ensuring alignment with business goals while minimizing risk throughout the transformation.
  • DevOps automation and continuous deployment pipelines, accelerating release cycles and improving code quality through automated testing and deployment processes.
  • Cloud-native security features with encryption and comprehensive auditing for compliance, protecting sensitive customer data and ensuring regulatory adherence.

Results Achieved

The cloud-native transformation delivered measurable benefits across operations, costs, and customer experience:

Accelerated Time-to-Market

Agile DevOps workflows enabled faster feature deployment and improved development velocity, allowing Falabella to respond quickly to market opportunities and customer needs.

Reduced Total Cost of Ownership

Cloud-first architecture significantly lowered infrastructure costs while improving system reliability and reducing maintenance overhead.

Improved Performance

Elastic scalability automatically matches business demand, ensuring optimal performance during peak shopping periods while optimizing resource utilization during normal operations.

Eliminated Operational Bottlenecks

Decentralized ownership and improved documentation reduced dependency risks and enabled teams to work more independently and efficiently.

Enhanced Customer Experience

Improved omnichannel capabilities deliver seamless shopping experiences across all devices, driving customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Future-Ready Infrastructure

Modern cloud-native architecture positions Falabella to quickly adopt emerging technologies and scale to meet future business demands.
