Client Overview

135+ years of retail leadership across 9 LATAM countries. Serves 36M+ customers through Falabella department stores, Sodimac, Tottus, Banco Falabella, and exclusive IKEA partnership.

90,000 employees driving innovation in retail, financial services, and e-commerce. Market-leading omnichannel player facing pressure to modernize legacy infrastructure while maintaining operational continuity.

The Challenge

Aging Architecture

Legacy Oracle ATG framework lacked flexibility for modern e-commerce. Slow feature releases. No agility to compete.

Infrastructure Chaos

Configuration files scattered across servers without version control. No change tracking. Risky deployments. Operational bottlenecks.

Knowledge & Cost Burdens

Critical expertise concentrated with few people. High infrastructure costs. Maintenance overhead blocking innovation.

The Solution

Pillar 1: Cloud-Native Infrastructure

Azure & Docker virtualization for scalable, reliable environments. Sprint-ready provisioning eliminates configuration drift and boosts developer velocity.

Pillar 2: DevOps Automation

Continuous deployment pipelines with automated testing. Fast release cycles. Enterprise-grade security with encryption and comprehensive auditing for compliance.

Pillar 3: Iterative & Low-Risk Execution

Phased rollout with frequent client validation. Collaborative planning ensures business alignment. Zero disruption to ongoing operations.
