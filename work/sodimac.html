Case Study

Sodimac Home Center Embraces Composable Commerce with commercetools Migration

NuLogic partnered with Latin America's leading home improvement retailer to migrate their e-commerce platform to commercetools, delivering enriched shopping experiences through modern composable commerce architecture.

Client

Sodimac Home Center

Region

Latin America

Industry

Home Improvement Retail

Solution Type

Platform Migration

Overview

NuLogic partnered with Sodimac Home Center to execute a comprehensive e-commerce transformation, migrating their digital platform to commercetools. As Latin America's leading home improvement retailer, Sodimac required a modern, flexible commerce solution capable of supporting their extensive product catalog, complex inventory systems, and multi-market operations.

About Sodimac Home Center

Sodimac operates as part of the Falabella Group and stands as the premier home improvement and construction retailer across Latin America. With hundreds of stores throughout Chile, Peru, Colombia, Argentina, and Uruguay, Sodimac serves millions of customers through both physical retail locations and digital commerce channels, offering everything from building materials and tools to home decor and furniture.

Key Challenges

Sodimac faced several critical business and technical challenges that required a comprehensive platform modernization:

Complex Product Catalog Management

Managing tens of thousands of SKUs across multiple categories, from small hardware items to large appliances and building materials, with varying attributes, pricing models, and inventory tracking requirements.

Legacy Platform Limitations

Existing e-commerce infrastructure lacked the flexibility and scalability needed to support modern customer experiences, omnichannel operations, and rapid feature deployment.

Multi-Market Complexity

Operating across multiple Latin American countries required support for different currencies, languages, tax regulations, and local payment methods while maintaining consistent brand experience.

Omnichannel Integration Requirements

Need to seamlessly connect digital commerce with physical store operations, including inventory visibility, buy online pickup in-store (BOPIS), and unified customer data.

Performance and Scalability

Platform needed to handle high traffic volumes during promotional events and seasonal peaks while maintaining fast page load times and responsive user experience.

Solutions Implemented

Composable Commerce Architecture

Implemented commercetools as the core commerce engine, enabling API-first, headless architecture that separates frontend presentation from backend commerce logic. This composable approach provides maximum flexibility for customization and integration.

Advanced Product Information Management

  • Migrated extensive product catalog with complex hierarchies and attributes
  • Implemented flexible product models supporting diverse product types
  • Configured multi-level categorization and faceted search capabilities
  • Enabled dynamic pricing and promotional rules across markets

Multi-Market and Multi-Channel Support

  • Configured commercetools for multiple countries with localized content
  • Implemented multi-currency pricing and checkout flows
  • Integrated local payment gateways for each market
  • Enabled region-specific tax calculations and compliance

Enterprise System Integration

Connected commercetools with Sodimac's enterprise ecosystem through robust API integrations:

  • ERP integration for real-time inventory synchronization
  • Order Management System (OMS) connectivity for unified fulfillment
  • PIM integration for centralized product data management
  • Customer data platform integration for personalized experiences
  • Store systems integration for BOPIS and inventory visibility

Enhanced Customer Experience Features

  • Personalized product recommendations and search
  • Advanced filtering and navigation for large catalogs
  • Project planning tools for home improvement projects
  • Store locator with real-time inventory availability
  • Professional contractor account management

Performance Optimization

Leveraged commercetools' cloud-native architecture and implemented best practices for optimal performance, including CDN integration, image optimization, and efficient API query patterns to ensure fast page loads even with extensive product catalogs.

Results Achieved

Successful Platform Migration

Completed migration of extensive product catalog, customer data, and order history to commercetools with minimal business disruption.

Enhanced Operational Agility

Composable architecture enables rapid deployment of new features, A/B testing, and market-specific customizations without platform constraints.

Improved Performance

Optimized page load times and site performance through cloud-native infrastructure and modern frontend architecture.

Omnichannel Excellence

Seamless integration between digital and physical channels, enabling unified inventory visibility and flexible fulfillment options.

Scalable Foundation

Modern commerce platform capable of supporting continued growth, new market expansion, and evolving customer expectations.

API-First Integration

Flexible architecture enabling easy integration with best-of-breed third-party services and future technology additions.

Business Impact

  • Enabled faster time-to-market for new features and promotional campaigns
  • Improved customer experience with personalized shopping and enhanced product discovery
  • Increased operational efficiency through streamlined workflows and automation
  • Positioned Sodimac for continued digital innovation and market leadership
  • Created foundation for advanced capabilities including AI-powered recommendations and virtual room planning
