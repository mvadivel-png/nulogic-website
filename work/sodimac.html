Case Study

Composable Commerce at Scale Across Latin America

Region

Latin America

Website

sodimac.com

Industry

Home Improvement Retail

Client Overview

Part of Falabella Group. Latin America's leading home improvement retailer.

500+ stores across 5 countries: Chile, Peru, Colombia, Argentina, Uruguay.

Omnichannel leader. Millions of customers shopping online and in-store daily.

The Challenge

Sodimac faced critical business and technical challenges requiring comprehensive platform modernization to support their vision for digital commerce excellence.

Key Challenges

Product & Catalog Complexity

Manage tens of thousands of SKUs with complex hierarchies, attributes, and pricing. Legacy systems couldn't handle the scale or flexibility needed for diverse product types across categories.

Multi-Market Operations

Operating across 5 countries demanded localized content, currencies, payment methods, and tax compliance—all while maintaining unified inventory and brand consistency across markets.

Omnichannel Integration

Connect 500+ physical stores with digital channels. Enable real-time inventory visibility, BOPIS, and unified customer data across all touchpoints without system silos.

The Solution

Pillar 1: Composable Commerce Architecture

Implemented commercetools as the core engine with API-first, headless architecture. Decoupled frontend from backend commerce logic, enabling maximum flexibility for customization, rapid iteration, and seamless third-party integrations without platform constraints.

Pillar 2: Enterprise Integrations & Data Sync

Connected ERP, OMS, PIM, and CDP systems through robust APIs for unified operations:

  • ✓ Real-time inventory synced across 500+ stores and digital channels
  • ✓ Unified fulfillment supporting BOPIS, store pickup, and home delivery
  • ✓ Personalization engine powered by unified customer data

Pillar 3: Multi-Market Scale & Performance

Built for growth across 5 countries with localized content, multi-currency pricing, region-specific payment gateways, and tax compliance. Cloud-native infrastructure with CDN, image optimization, and efficient APIs ensure blazing-fast performance even during peak traffic and seasonal spikes.

The Results

Zero Downtime Migration

Migrated millions of SKUs, customer records, and order history seamlessly without disrupting operations.

2x Feature Velocity

API-first architecture accelerated new feature launches and A/B testing across all 5 markets.

Sub-2s Page Load Times

Cloud-native infrastructure and optimization delivered significantly faster user experiences.

Complete Omnichannel Parity

Unified inventory visibility and BOPIS across all 500+ physical stores in real-time.

Infinitely Scalable

Platform handles millions of daily transactions and seasonal traffic spikes without degradation.

Personalization at Scale

AI-driven recommendations and search improve conversion rates across all markets.

Business Impact

  • ✓ Faster time-to-market: Deploy new features and campaigns in days, not weeks
  • ✓ Superior customer experience: Personalized browsing, faster checkout, unified fulfillment options
  • ✓ Operational excellence: Streamlined workflows, reduced manual processes, higher margins
  • ✓ Future-ready foundation: Extensible platform positioned for AI, new channels, and market expansion
