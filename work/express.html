The Challenge

Express is an apparel brand operating 400+ stores with e-commerce and mobile channels delivering modern style.

400+ Stores

1 Legacy Infrastructure

Aging AWS environment slowed releases and limited innovation velocity

2 Manual DevOps Bottlenecks
