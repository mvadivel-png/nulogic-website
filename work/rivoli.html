Case Study

Rivoli Group

A luxury lifestyle retailer operating 220+ stores across UAE, Oman, Qatar, and Bahrain. NuLogic provides managed commerce retainer services ensuring round-the-clock reliability and customer experience excellence.

Overview

The Rivoli Group is a luxury lifestyle retailer operating 220+ stores across UAE, Oman, Qatar, and Bahrain, representing 100+ international brands. Since 2023, NuLogic has served as their managed commerce partner, providing comprehensive platform support and optimization services.

Our managed commerce retainer service delivers round-the-clock monitoring, incident resolution, and strategic guidance to keep Rivoli's complex digital commerce ecosystem running at peak performance.

Engagement Details

  • Client: The Rivoli Group - Luxury lifestyle retailer with 220+ stores
  • Engagement Type: Managed commerce retainer service
  • Partnership Duration: Since 2023
  • Region: EMEA (UAE, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain)

Business Challenges

The Rivoli Group faced significant complexity managing their digital commerce ecosystem. Key challenges included:

  • Complex Stack Management: Managing a sophisticated technology stack including commercetools, Fluent Commerce OMS, and multiple supporting systems requiring continuous oversight
  • 24/7 Reliability Requirements: Need for round-the-clock reliability and customer experience excellence to support luxury retail operations
  • Global Support Coverage: Internal teams required dependable global off-hours commerce support to maintain operations
  • Evolving Customer Expectations: Necessity to keep pace with rapidly evolving customer expectations and emerging technologies

Solutions Implemented

NuLogic delivered comprehensive managed commerce support across multiple dimensions to address Rivoli's complex operational needs:

Platform Support

  • Real-time monitoring and incident resolution for commercetools
  • Performance stability optimization and operational efficiency improvements
  • Fluent Commerce OMS configuration and intelligent integrations

Customer Experience Enhancement

  • UI/UX redesign with focus on personalized search and advanced discovery
  • Strategic guidance on website refresh initiatives
  • Conversion rate optimization improvements

System Integration

  • Regular upgrades and seamless integration across connected systems
  • Omnichannel retail growth enablement
  • AI-powered merchandising automation

Support Model

  • Off-hours responsiveness capabilities

Results Achieved

Our managed commerce partnership with Rivoli Group has delivered measurable improvements across all key operational metrics:
