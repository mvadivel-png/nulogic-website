Case Study

El Palacio de Hierro

Accelerating Marketplace Success with Mirakl Marketplace Integration and Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC)

Overview

NuLogic transformed El Palacio de Hierro's digital commerce through Mirakl Marketplace Platform integration with Salesforce Commerce Cloud (SFCC), modernizing operations and enabling third-party seller expansion. This prestigious Mexican luxury department store, with over 135 years in premium retail, sought to blend traditional heritage with modern eCommerce innovation while attracting a younger, digitally-focused audience.

Client Background

  • Industry: Luxury Retail / Department Store
  • Location: Mexico City, Latin America
  • Heritage: Over 135 years in premium retail, recognized as a leader in Latin American digital commerce
  • Website: www.elpalaciodehierro.com

Business Challenges

El Palacio de Hierro faced several critical challenges in their digital transformation journey:

Primary Issues

  • Need for rapid evolution beyond traditional eCommerce
  • Requirement to launch a robust, scalable third-party marketplace
  • Complex integration of Mirakl with SFCC using Storefront Reference Architecture (SFRA)
  • Risk of data synchronization failures between platforms
  • Manual overhead from import/export processes
  • Scalability concerns for growing seller networks

Key Pain Points

  • Product category, attribute, inventory, and pricing mismatches
  • Manual workflow inefficiencies
  • Performance risks with storefront stability

Solution Implemented

NuLogic deployed prebuilt connector cartridges (bm_mirakl, int_mirakl, int_mirakl_sfra) creating seamless Mirakl-SFCC connectivity through a comprehensive multi-phase strategy:

1. Cloud Integration for Real-Time Sync

  • Automated API orchestration
  • Continuous data exchange across product catalogs, pricing, inventory
  • Accuracy assurance across platforms

2. Scalable Architecture Mapping

  • Flexible catalog and attribute frameworks
  • Cross-platform alignment
  • Foundation for third-party marketplace growth

3. Storefront Optimization with SFRA

  • Seamless display of third-party product data
  • Accurate pricing and imagery
  • Superior customer experience enhancement

4. Workflow Automation

  • Robust job management
  • Automatic data synchronization
  • Reduced manual overhead and error minimization

5. Performance Tuning

  • API refactoring
  • Optimized job execution
  • Site speed and reliability safeguarding

Results & Outcomes

The implementation delivered significant measurable improvements and positioned El Palacio de Hierro as a digitally innovative luxury retailer:

Time-to-Market

Accelerated marketplace launch through prebuilt e-commerce integrations and automation

Data Accuracy

Real-time synchronization of products, offers, and pricing across multiple sellers

Platform Scalability

Modular architecture supporting effortless expansion and long-term growth

Customer Experience

Wider product selection, accurate availability, faster discovery driving engagement and conversions

Market Position

Recognition as innovative e-commerce leader with robust, future-ready online marketplace

Strategic Impact

The implementation enabled El Palacio de Hierro to achieve marketplace scalability without operational disruption, enhanced competitive positioning in Latin American eCommerce, and established a foundation for sustainable revenue growth through third-party seller network expansion, all while maintaining their brand heritage.
