Agentic Commerce Case Study

Leslie's Pool Supplies

Deploying autonomous AI agents to optimize seasonal inventory, predict demand patterns, and automate stock replenishment across 900+ locations.

Overview

Leslie's Pool Supplies, North America's largest specialty pool retailer with 900+ locations, faced critical inventory challenges due to highly seasonal demand patterns. Pool chemicals, equipment, and accessories have limited shelf life and dramatic seasonal fluctuations, leading to costly stockouts during peak summer months and excessive inventory waste during off-seasons.

NuLogic deployed autonomous AI agents to revolutionize Leslie's inventory management, demand forecasting, and supply chain operations. Our agentic commerce solution continuously learns from weather patterns, regional pool seasons, historical sales data, and real-time demand signals to automatically optimize stock levels across all locations.

The Challenge

  • Seasonal Demand Volatility: Pool supply demand varies dramatically by season, region, and weather conditions, making traditional forecasting unreliable
  • Perishable Inventory: Pool chemicals have expiration dates, requiring precise inventory timing to minimize waste
  • Multi-Location Complexity: 900+ stores across different climates and pool seasons required localized inventory strategies
  • Stock Imbalances: Frequent stockouts during peak season and 30%+ excess inventory during off-season
  • Manual Processes: Inventory managers spending 60+ hours per week on manual forecasting and ordering

The Agentic Commerce Solution

NuLogic implemented an autonomous AI agent system that operates 24/7 to manage Leslie's entire inventory lifecycle:

  • Predictive Demand Agents: AI agents analyze weather forecasts, regional pool opening/closing patterns, historical sales data, and local events to predict demand with 95%+ accuracy
  • Autonomous Replenishment Agents: Agents automatically generate purchase orders, negotiate with suppliers, and coordinate deliveries based on predicted demand
  • Smart Redistribution Agents: AI identifies inventory imbalances across locations and autonomously initiates inter-store transfers to optimize stock levels
  • Expiration Management Agents: Monitors chemical shelf life and automatically triggers promotions or redistribution before products expire
  • Dynamic Pricing Agents: Adjusts prices in real-time based on inventory levels, demand forecasts, and competitive landscape
