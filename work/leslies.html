Case Study

NULogic Boosts Leslie's Digital Experience with Faster Site Speed, Stronger Customer Loyalty, and Mobile Optimization

NULogic transformed Leslie's digital experience by boosting website speed, optimizing mobile performance, enhancing customer loyalty, and driving higher engagement.

REGION

U.S.

WEBSITE

lesliespool.com

CLIENT SINCE

2023

Company Overview

Founded in 1963, Leslie's represents the largest and most trusted pool and spa care brand across the United States. The company operates over 1,000 retail locations and maintains a robust eCommerce platform, delivering an omnichannel experience that encompasses pool supplies, spa products, water treatment solutions, and expert services available through multiple channels.

The Challenge

The engagement revealed seven critical obstacles:

  • Misalignment among development, QA, and design teams slowing eCommerce delivery and increasing project risks
  • Scalability difficulties during peak shopping periods on the SFCC platform
  • Slow page load times stemming from oversized JavaScript and CSS bundles affecting Core Web Vitals
  • Elevated API latency reducing site responsiveness and customer satisfaction
  • Suboptimal mobile user experience requiring faster, optimized digital journeys
  • Unstable mobile workflows creating inconsistent performance
  • Fragmented loyalty program experience diminishing customer re-engagement

The Solution

Backend Refactoring for Performance

  • Refactored SFCC controllers using Salesforce Commerce Cloud best practices
  • Reduced API latency and improved backend responsiveness
  • Rebuilt modular integrations using Boomi middleware for stability

Frontend & Mobile Optimization

  • Improved Lighthouse performance scores from sub-50s to mid-80s
  • Eliminated render-blocking resources and compressed JS/CSS bundles
  • Streamlined SFCC ⇄ Boomi ⇄ Mobile data flows
  • Optimized SFCC eCDN with advanced caching strategies

Figma-Led Product Engineering

  • Designed UX flows in Figma, converted into JIRA sprint plans
  • Delivered cross-platform development with full-stack coverage
  • Applied engineering processes used by global leaders like Adidas and L'Oréal

Field Services Experience Redesign

  • Rebuilt Service Appointment Portal with dynamic Q&A logic
  • Integrated custom SFCC objects with Accruent via Boomi APIs
  • Delivered seamless booking experience for customers and field staff

Loyalty 2.0: Pool Perks Go Tiered

  • Transformed flat-point rewards system into tiered loyalty program
  • Integrated third-party rewards platforms via real-time APIs
  • Enabled live loyalty points tracking and redemption visibility

Checkout Overhaul + GA4 Integration

  • Redesigned entire checkout experience for higher conversions
  • Embedded Google Analytics 4 for cookieless tracking
  • Positioned Leslie's for future-proof customer analytics

Support & Monitoring Infrastructure

NULogic embedded continuous monitoring and DevOps automation:

  • Dynatrace Real User Monitoring (RUM): Real-time behavioral mapping of customer journeys
  • Opsgenie Alerts: Instant escalation to engineering teams, cutting downtime risk
  • SLA-Driven JIRA Workflows: High-priority incident resolution under 15 minutes
  • Cross-Platform Database Monitoring: Unified visibility across SFCC, Boomi, and Manhattan OMS
