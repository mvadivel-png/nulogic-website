Partnership Overview

NULogic partners with Shopware, a holistic omnichannel digital commerce platform founded in 2000. The platform serves mid-market and lower enterprise merchants with headless, API-first architecture using Open-Source design. It supports B2C, D2C, B2B, and service-based use cases, particularly strong in the DACH region and growing in North America.

Services Offered
