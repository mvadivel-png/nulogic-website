El Desafío

As one of the largest retailers in Chile, Walmart needed a reliable support partner to manage their complex commerce platform operations. Key challenges included:

  • Round-the-clock availability: Required 24/7/365 support coverage for critical e-commerce operations
  • Rapid incident response: Needed expert L2/L3 support engineers to quickly diagnose and resolve complex technical issues
  • Platform stability: Maintain high uptime and performance across peak traffic periods and promotional events
  • Bilingual support: Required Spanish and English-speaking engineers familiar with LATAM market requirements
