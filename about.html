We're Building the Future of Commerce Starting Today

Since 2008, NULogic has grown from a small team of engineers into a 250+ person commerce partner you can count on, with teammates across the US, Canada, Mexico, Chile, and India.

Who We Are

We're a commerce engineering and managed services firm that does one thing and we do it well: retail and e-commerce. That focus matters. It means we bring real-world expertise, not just theory, to every project helping retailers run platforms that actually work.

Here's how we started: our founders were engineers and commerce platform experts who saw too many retailers struggling with partners who didn't truly understand the business of commerce. They knew retailers needed someone who got both the technology and the business side so they built NULogic.

Years in the trenches taught us what really works. We've built and run commerce platforms for major retailers, and today we bring that hard-earned experience to every project.

We work with enterprise retailers, DTC brands, and multi-brand organizations around the world. What we do is simple, but critical: implement platforms that launch on time, provide 24/7 managed support when issues arise, engineer for high performance, and leverage data and AI to give businesses a competitive edge.

Retailers like Walmart trust us with their mission-critical commerce platforms and that trust is something we earn every single day.
