Building the Future of Digital Commerce Today

Founded in 2008, NuLogic has evolved into a trusted digital commerce partner with over 250 skilled professionals distributed across the US, Canada, Mexico, Chile, and India.

Who We Are

NuLogic is a commerce engineering and managed services firm focused exclusively on retail and e-commerce. We provide engineering expertise and strategic guidance to retail and eCommerce companies pursuing digital transformation.

Founded by engineers and commerce platform experts, NuLogic was built on a simple belief: retailers need partners who understand both the technology and the business of commerce. We've spent years building and running commerce platforms for major retailers, and we bring that expertise to every engagement.

Today, we work with enterprise retailers, DTC brands, and multi-brand retail organizations across the globe, delivering implementations, 24/7 managed support, performance engineering, and data/AI capabilities. We're trusted by companies like Walmart and other leading retailers to run mission-critical commerce platforms and deliver results that matter.
